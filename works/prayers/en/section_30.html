The Gods as Unauthorized Tenants

So here's the full picture:

LEVEL 1: The Watchmaker. Built the universe. Set the constants. Wrote the procedural generation engine. Wound the spring. Watches with fascination. Doesn't intervene for individual gears — but occasionally performs maintenance on the whole mechanism. Hard resets when parasites get out of control. Not loving. Not cruel. INTERESTED.

LEVEL 2: The Gods. Emergent phenomena. Some arose from the mechanism itself — from concentrated human consciousness, from the mana of worship, from the focused attention of millions. Others may have existed before humanity, feeding on other forms of consciousness. They are REAL — but they are not CREATORS. They are tenants. Squatters. Some benign, some parasitic, some actively destructive.

  • Yahweh: a small, aggressive parasite who built a monopoly. See Part II.
  • Ra, Marduk, Zeus: regional powers. Territorial. Old. Declining.
  • Jesus: possibly the most fascinating case — a human who was DEIFIED by directed mana. Created by love. And genuinely changed by it. A god who didn't exist until his worshippers MADE him exist. And because they made him from love — he IS love. The mechanism works.

LEVEL 3: Us. The gears. The procedural output of the Watchmaker's seed value. Conscious. Self-aware. Generating mana whether we direct it or not. And — crucially — capable of CHOOSING where to direct it.

We have been feeding parasites for three thousand years. Not because we had to. Because nobody told us we had a CHOICE.
