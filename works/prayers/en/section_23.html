Part III: The Indifferent Watchmaker

So who, after all, "created the Heavens and the Earth"? We've already established that Yahweh certainly had nothing to do with it — and, by all appearances, neither did any of humanity's other gods.

Gods are nothing more than collective egregores of peoples, or parasitic entities that managed to park themselves on the mana stream from believers. I don't mean to say they're all bad. Jesus himself (whom I suspect will need a dedicated article in this series, and who appears to be of the greatest interest as one of the few documented cases of DEIFICATION — and if you think that directing an enormous stream of faith at a LIVING PERSON won't produce results, think again) — he's actually a benevolent God who gave Yahweh no end of grief with his appearance and essentially forced him to reinvent his cult in the form of aggressive, expansionist Islam.

But the problem isn't that.
