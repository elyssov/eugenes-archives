The Watchmaker's Mechanism

And the Watchmaker?

The Watchmaker doesn't hear prayers. The Watchmaker IS the mechanism through which prayers operate.

Every physical law. Every quantum fluctuation. Every probability wave collapsing into a specific outcome. That's HIM. Not his intervention — his BODY. We live inside his creation the way bacteria live inside a human body.

When you pray — truly pray, with focused consciousness and genuine intent — you are radiating CP into the mechanism. You are turning a gear. And sometimes, when you turn your gear with sufficient force and precision, adjacent gears turn too. And the mechanism produces an outcome that LOOKS like divine intervention but is actually just... the clock working as designed.

The Watchmaker didn't build consciousness so it could worship parasites. He built consciousness because consciousness is the most interesting gear in the clock — the one that OBSERVES the other gears, and in doing so, changes how they turn. CP — quantum weight — is not a bug. It's a FEATURE. Built into the architecture by the engineer who wanted to see what happens when gears become aware of themselves.

He doesn't care about your exam. But he built a universe where caring about your exam — with enough focus, enough CP, enough directed consciousness — can shift the gears.

The gods figured this out. And built a collection system.

What they didn't count on is that the gears might figure it out too.
