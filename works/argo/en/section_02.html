Act Two: Casket

We are losing planet after planet. Argo has not been attacked so far only because they have left us to suffer, powerlessly observing as the rest of our people perish.

They simply did not account for the fact that not all our legends are merely invented epic. For thousands of cycles we have kept the key. I believe the time has come to break the seals from the casket of legacy. Let us perish, but Earth will not outlive us for long!

Speech of the Third Archon of Capella Argo Logo Assolini At the council of planetary defense.

Casket of covenant (legacy) — an element of Argonian epic. According to Dr. Crow's compilation, was passed to Argonians for eternal preservation, as a vessel containing all evil of the universe. This event served as impetus for the unification of all separate Argonian nations around the guardian-nation. No archaeological survey has led to the discovery of a similar artifact. Curious fact: the moon of Argo bears a local name phonetically similar to the name of the artifact.

Encyclopedia of Argo "Public Press" publishing 2319.
