Chapter 2

It took Vlad half an hour to get home, though usually it took five minutes. He looked into windows, kicked cars, making sirens scream like fools... There were no people.

...Electricity, however, was in the network, and the internet, for the moment at least, hadn't gone anywhere either. Though it didn't make things much easier: while the internet lived, RuNet seemed to be dead. Not a single message on "Lenta" since three in the morning. Not a single message on LiveJournal, Facebook and Twitter — except foreign accounts. Vlad reached for the keyboard to drum out a panicked message, but at the last moment pulled his fingers back: whatever it was, it hadn't touched him. Where the residents of his district (he didn't allow himself to think bigger) and the inhabitants of RuNet had vanished was unclear, but in any case he had no desire to vanish himself. If it was the use of some new weapon, the survivors might want to be finished off too. Or caught and studied.

On the other hand, living people clearly still existed — a friend from the USA posted that he'd lost the Hearthstone tournament and gone to bed. It remained to understand what to do next.

In any case, the fact that there were no people left needed checking, and for that one had to prepare. Armed with a sledgehammer dug out of the niche behind the toilet tank, Vlad headed for Auchan.

Already before the doors, the lad hesitated. After all, civilisation-instilled skills and rules are hard to just throw aside. Nevertheless, making an effort, he struck the blow...

...The first person Vlad found literally beyond the Auchan doors. Slightly to the side, in a shapeless heap of rags, lay a security guard's uniform, with belt, radio, and for some reason several teeth on top. Forcing himself, Vlad looked closer — the teeth were clearly an artificial "bridge"...

He found five more such "heaps" on the way to the actual shopping floor. Looks like people had vanished "in the buff." All foreign objects ended up in heaps of rags. For some reason he remembered a classmate, Ira Motovilina, the first beauty of their year, already running around with some "sugar daddy": it became curious — would two bags of silicone lie in the heap left from her? From these thoughts Vlad became disgusted with himself.
