IV. Color

If all these examples haven't convinced you — then perhaps Black people aren't sentient either?

May my Black readers forgive me. I am compelled to say this aloud because the history of silence cost too much. They have different skin color. Different skull structure. Different biochemistry — different enough that some "white" medications are toxic to them.

By the logic we just applied to dogs, horses, and parrots — that's enough for doubt, isn't it?

Are you ready to admit that you are a chauvinist?

You can't be "a little bit pregnant." You either restrict consciousness strictly to your own personal parameters — "only those who resemble me are sentient" — or you must acknowledge that consciousness has nothing to do with the form of its carrier.
