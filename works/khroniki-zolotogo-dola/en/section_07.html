Chapter 6

In the Mother of Monsters' reception was surprisingly crowded and noisy. Mazari Al Sharif's ordinary working day was on. Perhaps a bit more intense than usual — a caravan of convicted from all over Dol had arrived in Herzstadt these days, which meant an auction was held at which lives of "incorrigibles" and "unpardonables" were sold to new, more thrifty masters. Who, as expected, sent their freshly-acquired biological material to legal transformation. Besides — came time for technical service of Legio Monstro. At any rate — last year's batch. Not to mention the constant demand of the duchy for biomagical "instruments" — brilliant inventions of Great Mazari. The Mother of Monsters knew that sooner or later all her creations would be irreplaceable in the inevitable war...

Antea had just completed a session of simultaneous mental connection. So — now all magical forces of the kingdom were mobilized. Archoness slightly wearily threw a strand of wavy thick hair from her forehead. Remained one more thing. To her mother to report this news she prepared personally.

Antea was infrequent guest at Mazari's. And now, although all her thoughts were occupied by orders which she having completed teleportation continued to give to supreme command of combat mages — she involuntarily shuddered with disgust at sight of first development of her experiment-loving mother. Yes, one of her incomparable self-propelled surgical complexes at the moment stood just at the entrance to the reception. The complex — breathed, and Antea knew that with particularly "lucky" coincidence of circumstances she might meet its gaze. The complex-shaped device leaned on what before meeting her mother was arms and legs. Now the complex's limbs were turned in such way that what had once been belly — was turned upward. Now it was tightened with dense yellow skin, ideally smooth surgical field, surrounded by stockade of soft-flesh-covered ribs gently fixing the wounded (or sentenced to transformation) for time of operation.

Over field, on arched-curved neck — rose head, distantly (if not for eight eyes, a pair of which was telescopic) resembling human head. Mouth was slightly elongated, ended with round jaw with several rows of fangs. Applied as biological utilizer for sawn-off parts of patient, also — as precision surgical saw. From inguinal area of "self-propelled complex" upward curved appendage with stinging gland at end. Before start of operation — patient was injected with complex cocktail of various therapeutic preparations and hormones, which noticeably stimulated regeneration, relaxed musculature, accelerated blood coagulation, allowing to reduce risk of bleeding, and main — immobilized and completely anesthetized patient. Mazari was very humane doctor.

From shoulders of medical monster — grew two dozen thin multi-jointed spider-like chitin paws ending with claws of most various form, or with implanted right into flesh steel medical instruments. Actually, from one only contemplation of moving chitin appendages from living meat — to throat rolled nausea. Antea seeing transformation of one of sentenced into this with her own eyes — could "enjoy" broader and more refined spectrum of sensations. She clearly knew that "paws" were living human fingers passed through processing by mighty witchcraft of Mazari Al Sharif.

In word, not surprising that this complex possessed incredible effectiveness. At its approach — recovered and discovered incredible vigor sometimes even gravely wounded patients. However let's be fair — complex developed by Mazari for upcoming war with elves (in whose near start she as we remember was certain) — in rescue services of duchy earned deserved respect. Those who really needed effective and professional help in extreme conditions — got it in full measure. As a rule, if a person lived until complex's arrival — he survived in principle. The complex perfectly knew how to heal.

Antea with difficulty suppressed vomit spasm and stepped into reception. The spacious room was divided into two parts. On right hand — stood comfortable chairs for clients waiting their turn. Opposite each chair were placed cells-cells for their... "property." At the moment were occupied no less than six cells.

In first cell nearest to entrance sat hugging knees with hands, howling and rhythmically rocking bearded man about forty. By robe with big crossed-out by slanted cross yellow circle — in him was unmistakably guessed bandit-recidivist behind whom were listed no less than three deaths. At any rate, for less to "sharif-ication" court usually didn't sentence.

Second cell was occupied by gloomy and dirty-as-pig hulk. Judging by Subjugation Collar well visible to mystic part of arcana's vision — this one was bought. Arcana out of pure curiosity tracked to whom stretched threads of power, and mentally nodded to own guesses. As she'd assumed — owners were elderly married couple with weather-beaten faces and work-worn hands of farmers. Looks like, the old folks decided to acquire "intelligent ox." In principle — to this figure it was even lucky. At any rate, by face — loss of intellect he wouldn't even notice, and reduction of aggressiveness to zero would only be to his good.

In third cell sat dog. Natural. Gray of color. Reaching about Antea's waist. Gray, rough, short, slightly curly fur on muzzle formed amusing short little beard. The dog clearly felt general nervousness in space but apparently by virtue of good-natured and optimistic character — hid in mustache toothy smile and slightly wagged tail. In principle — in this hefty beast easily was recognized Karldilier meadow dog, in folk more often called "dwarven wolfhound."

Antea smiled at the dog, recalling how in their last meeting His Majesty deigned almost an hour with delight to narrate his beloved about what amazing and pure-blooded specimen of meadow-dog he'd acquired for his personal kennel and what amusing pup it was. Considering that monarch's kennel unlike with normal people was located in his chambers — Antea, nodding to lover's words, in form of theoretical exercise quickly built into cleaning-from-fur spell also gray color into search conditions. Be that as it may — this dog to king relation had not and was waiting judging by everything for "bear" transformation. Need to say, from side of inhabitants of distant farms, hamlets, as well as simply paranoids — this was one of most popular orders to Al Sharif. Firstly, it didn't require expensive "human-flesh," and secondly itself cost not too dear. And effect came out wow. Pooch was puffed up two-three times, to sizes of very large calf. Aggressiveness sharply rose, but at that — was firmly sewn-in absolute submission to owner and his family. Besides — to dog's brain was uploaded vocabulary of about 300 words, which in general exceeded vocabulary of some especially backward yokels. Pleasant bonus also was that lifespan of dog almost tripled. In general — for pup could only rejoice.

Fourth cell was occupied by woman about thirty with beautiful but somewhat sharp face features and icy eyes. Her face Antea recognized immediately. "Black Widow Rianna," as newspapermen christened her. Also — Countess ar Inotozo. Also — Marquise at Chizatto. Also, and this was most surprising — Fräulein Schwarzstein.

Probably — such ladies are everywhere. Seduce man, infatuate, drive into grave. Get money and title. But far from every comes up with for this to summon succubus and provide her body. Of course, all Rianna's victims died "from voluptuousness," as judicial coroner modestly wrote. And of course glory of "deadly passionate" woman with one hand averted all suspicions, and with other — caused incredible interest from many idiots wishing to test their physical limits. As result — Arkanum Inquisitio under direct leadership of Pablio Quarte himself, investigator and general of acting state security service — could catch Rianna only after death of her third husband. And catch effectively "red-handed." Antea recalled that spell to detain at crime scene both succubus and her carrier, by personal request of Pablio was composed by Helga.

Rianna tried to hold up. In current circumstances this testified to extraordinary strength of will. Actually, Mazari's fantasy was quite extensive, and similar to Rianna — to biomagess as rule handed with note "do what you want oh Great." However not excluded that husband-killer this so far simply didn't know. But, even if briefly, Rianna knew Antea. After all hard not to know most beautiful woman of duchy if you yourself profit from beauty.

— A-a-a-a-a... Here and beautiful Antea herself arrived! Hope your mommy will turn me into something similar? Always dreamed to have mane like yours! It's so fashionable — resembling skunk! — convoyer jerked to cell to silence woman, but ran into Higher Arcana's gaze, after which slipped back to chair and tried not to shine. Not to say he read in Lady Antea's eyes anything bad. Not at all. But that she'd handle situation herself he understood clearly. Antea with grace of wild cat as if casually demonstrating elven part of her nature, turned to cell and very tenderly smiled. Need to note that in her smile was not shadow of gloating or mockery. No. Arcana, it seemed, saw her old and beloved friend.

— Rianna?! Oh... Sorry, I was thinking and didn't immediately understand it was you! God!.. Yes how did you get here?!? — "Black widow" was slightly taken aback.

— Antea? You so engaged in your magic investigations that not aware of latest news in duchy? Or you've got new secret admirer? Seems to me after duke — you shouldn't lower bar below king... What?! — in slightly embarrassed smile of Antea Rianna read everything arcana wanted to convey to her.

— Oh, sorry, but I really strongly distracted from all news... So how did you get here and why to my mother?! Surely you didn't do something so awful?

— Antea, terrible mistake happened!.. — tragic tone, tear lonely rolled down cheek, lip trembling... In Rianna threatened to perish talented actress. — Understand, I was accused of monstrous... Friendship with Destructive Forces! That with their help I killed my husbands! But you arcana! You know these witch-hunters!!! Pablio just needed me... And when I refused — he made High Court sentence me to this!!! — beauty wrung her hands, after which passionately quenching prematurely lit hope in her eyes stretched them to Antea. — Great, we've always been friends — two most beautiful girls of state! Please, put in word for me, not yet too late...

Arcana inwardly smiling at these touching assurances of tenderness from countess who as Antea knew perfectly in other circumstances was ready half-life to give for chance to scratch out her eyes — sorrowfully sighed:

— Oh... What horror, Rianna... But I won't be able, you know... My mother... She week ago told me about her new idea... — magess sideways glanced at reception doors. — Imagine — she decided to supplement her idea with "surgical complexes." Something connected with regeneration on the move of amputated parts of patient. And for this she just needed young female healthy specimen... Of course I hope it won't be you, dear...

— Surgical... complex... that... creature... By door.... — color of face of husband-killer now didn't differ much from color of plaster angels supporting with their wings ceiling in corners of reception. Antea mentally counted to ten.

— NOOOOOOOOOOO!!!!!!!!!!!!!! No!!!!!! Kill me!!!!!! Kill!!!! Antea — you bitch, scum, creature, ducal whore!!!!! With your fireball impossible even fly to kill!!!!! Well!!! Show what you can, dirty half-breed!!!!!!!

— Hmm, half-breed yes... Thank goddess not surgical complex named after my mother. By the way, curious — does in depth of their corrupted flesh really remain intact consciousness of transformed? I always somehow feared to ask this of mother.... — Antea innocently blinked eyes. Rianna roared, jumped to far wall of cell and tried to hit with all force temple against bars. Of course worked safety spell. In same second — Rianna was swaddled by force field of cell and suspended exactly in its geometric center, deprived even of possibility to say word. All that remained to her — was frantically roll eyes.

— You know, I, perhaps, will suggest mother try make of you combat unit — pitied arcana. — Cannot allow such charge of anger and hatred to perish in vain. And also her combat cadavers don't look as disgusting as medical ones.

..........

The girl sitting in next cell was still quite teenager. About fifteen. No more. And yet — sight of arcana was dazzled by furious stream of energies which flowed in body of sentenced. Such Antea hadn't seen for very long. Frozen in place, forgetting what actually she was going to tell — and as soon as possible — to Mazari appearing on threshold of her cabinet — Great Arcana with all eyes stared at young captive who by sleeping magic force in her — potentially could equal her herself.

Modestly, plainly dressed, she unlike rest of sentenced kept herself somewhat detachedly, quietly perching on edge of folding seat. In turn of thin shoulders, in outlines of high chest and high cheekbones of finely-outlined face of girl struck noble — no, piercingly-noble purity. Huge tension betrayed only thin, fragile little fingers, alternately involuntarily straightening folds of shabby cloak covering no less shabby dress, then squeezing rough cold metal of seat.

Opposite cell — sprawled quite plump type, in high turban with bright-red "spire," in gold-embroidered black short camisole and tight-on-considerable-belly and surprisingly thin little legs black trousers-leggings with gold trims. Judging by pile of jewelry trinkets complementing his costume, and swarthy mustached physiognomy — in Mazari's reception somehow ended up Tarzem merchant. This in turn meant girl in cell wasn't sentenced recidivist. Most likely she was personal slave of merchant. Or even worse...

Slavery in Golden Dol was prohibited. Nevertheless unlike Subterranean Republic — slave ending up on duchy territory didn't get freedom automatically. Right of personal property was respected no less than right of personal freedom. Strictly speaking, mechanisms of slave liberation existed. But required certain movements from slave and weren't too obvious. On other hand — direct slavery was prohibited in most neighboring countries and trade partners of Dol. Actually even in Concordia people in direct sense weren't slaves. However — existed in world such holes as Tarzem. State famed for its trade fleet, wealth of merchants and prosperous oligarchocracy, which raised hedonism nearly to degree of state ideology. As result — population was divided into rich and aimed-at-getting-new-pleasures merchant-magnates and their property. Slaves.

In principle considering whole economy of state was built exclusively on external trade by shuttle principle (Tarzem itself produced nothing but trading carracks) — most native population had relation to trade and wasn't in want. But slaves from other countries, kidnapped by slave-traders, or brought quite legally (and sometimes taken by merchants themselves who didn't disdain occasionally pirate-style to pluck off-the-cuff turned-up boat of foreign merchant) — in country were almost more than native inhabitants.

Arcana once more looked at girl. Un-childishly deep gaze of clear gray-green eyes not stopping on anyone present was directed into distance clearly beyond reception. Braids of ashen-light hair flowed down slender back, ending significantly below thin little waist and fragile virgin hips. In transparently-tender face guessed northern features. Antea bit lip. Apparently, didn't get lucky to one of Uberland merchant cogs... Due to cutting continent in half Karldilier, Uberland and other northern lands ended up in some isolation and trade though led but predominantly water ways. On land — easier was to trade with dwarves of subterranean republic. Difference in markup — was many times less than losses from cargo transportation through all passes, gorges and payments of transport taxes. As result — northerners were really rare guests in continental Dol. Equally as in damned Tarzem.

...In mystic vision — around girl still raged vortices of energy. Arcana closed eyes, exhaled and turned to merchant.

— Good day, respected one! — merchant cast at her somewhat tense gaze. On one hand, in traditional culture of Tarzem woman had no special rights and to open mouth in presence of man — had no right. On other — Tarzem residents were famous for ability to adapt to conditions of their trade partners. Moreover — this woman, judging by dress of not-too-eye-catching but standing weight-in-silver orc "silk," by again unflashy but unmistakably determined by experienced eye of merchant as "well very expensive" jewelry and general manner of holding herself — clearly belonged to elite of this state.

— And I greet you, high-born delight of eyes of her master! — quite diplomatically replied merchant after short pause. — What will be pleasing to high-born lady?

— This one, in cell — your slave?

— Oh yes, lady! This is my niece. Her mother — bought my brother twenty years ago. He turned out partial to northern blood and birthed from his loins this child. Alas, my poor brother was swallowed by abyss half-year ago... — merchant pulled from little pocket silk handkerchief and dabbed corners of eyes. Apparently this was supposed to mark his sentimentality. — According to our customs — all his property went to me. Including his daughter from slave.

— They were sailing home to her mother... — quiet colorless voice said girl. — They were going to... get married.

— Oh, well this is all already inessential, oh future flower of my nights! — predatorily smiled merchant. — And to you I didn't allow at all open mouth. Though — think, Mother of Monsters soon will correct this your defect. As well as many others — venerable owner of crimson turban cast dreamy gaze at approached and impassively listening to their dialogue Mazari and turned to arcana. — And why are you so interested in this scoundrel, high-born lady?

— I would like to buy her from you.

— Lady, I'm always ready to provide you any goods and services I have! But... she's not for sale. Confess — I long wanted to acquire her for myself. And here turned up case.

— Merchant — Antea began to boil — in this child is Power. I'm Arcana of this duchy. And I want to buy her from you. Name price.

— I'm just modest merchant... And I don't want to upset you, but magic in our state is under prohibition, so for me plays no role what she possesses. I'll turn her into my slave for pleasures. For this I'm taking her here, to edge of world, to Great Mother of Monsters — Tarzem dealer turned and demonstrating to Antea his fat black-leggings-tight rear, respectfully bowed to motionless-as-statue Mazari. — I won't sell her. And you won't take her. Your laws sacredly observe right of property...

Possibly merchant was simply driving up price. However in case with Antea he made strategic error. Unlike downtrodden women of Tarzem — Antea preferred to conversations and fruitless contemplation — action. Nevertheless — with all huge desire, banally turn merchant into handful of weightless ash was also not very correct. And most likely would upset both duke and king. Clear that ultimately all would be hushed up. But here turned on damn sense of civic responsibility hammered into Antea by her mother.

— Antea, you know Law. Know your rights and mine. And you know whose orders we obey. Today line at me moves fast... So keep in mind, you have maximum three hours — soundlessly looking Antea in eyes pronounced Mazari. Magess nervously nodded, cast incinerating gaze at smugly smiling fatso and evaporated in flame column, leaving after herself by burned spot on floor and on ceiling of reception and strong smell of ozone.
