Chapter 4

While Artex dines, Duke Mark Ariette sets in motion the rather stagnant machinery of his military machine, and Hans Hermstag prepares to support allies with all forces — we, probably, should learn more about the elves.

Actually, the elves were the youngest race of Alkirke. They appeared in their time in quantity of about 10 thousand individuals on the shore of the Red Ocean (so dubbed for the specific luminous plankton loving to dangle as foam near shore, providing the population with excellent free protein product), on the west of Manor continent, on which the duchy of Golden Dol we are examining was located. And along with it — recall! — the whole kingdom of Ksarpathia. Well, that very kingdom of which the duchy was a part...

Initially King Tailon, on whose territory the long-eared landing appeared — even rejoiced. After all subjects' addition, and what subjects! The newly-arisen inhabitants of kingdom Samris, where Tailon then ruled — attracted attention not only by ears. Elves were stately and tall handsomes with athletic builds. In strength an average elf even somewhat exceeded an average human. In reflex speed, reactions, gracefulness — left far behind. Plasticity of gymnast, might of ancient warrior and elusive lightness of movements combined in one body — captivated and impressed. If elves compared to people were distinguished by higher stature and stronger build — then beautiful elf-maidens, on the contrary, struck by their elegance and were even somewhat more miniature than human ladies. Flexible figures of these weightless creations were distinguished by the fact that with all their miniature size possessed quite worthy roundnesses everywhere where they followed, and not few human women had every chance to envy elf-maidens in size of these very assets. Well and of course pleasant surprise was the fact that besides purely biological merits — elves immediately recommended themselves as magnificent master-craftsmen.

In general, Tailon rejoiced. True not for long. No, at first all was quiet. Elves examined surroundings and absorbed peculiarities of new place. With native population tried not to contact extra. Moreover obeyed all local rules and even sacrificed at first demand two prettier and curvier elf-maidens into the king's personal harem. By the way — for the impressed-by-their-charms king at that moment arose the idea of making himself a half-elf heir. However, to his great chagrin — biological incompatibility came up. No, undoubtedly, he tried very hard, lucky that anatomically elves from people, as turned out, weren't particularly different. But crossbreeding somehow didn't work out. Moreover the matter wasn't even in the peculiarity of elven biology (and as turned out subsequently, peculiarity indeed was — and not small!) Simply human and elf really were different species. Like bear and wolf, for example.

While the king engaged in eugenics, and his advisors thought what to do with the bunch of new arriving citizens, elves bustled in their amazingly quickly erected settlement day and night, constantly building something there, raising beautiful temples (apparently to establish lost connection with their gods) and for some reason planting a whole grove of young and strong trees not far from their settlement. And exactly three years later, on Night of Arrival, as they called this holiday afterwards, the elves went on offensive. Then it turned out that craftsmanship — turned into elegant and at same time sharp-as-razor one-and-a-half-handed slightly curved swords with beveled point, with which (two each per nose) elves shredded human infantry with speed and effectiveness of dwarven meat-grinder on steam drive. And besides craft talents — the long-eared also had everything in order with magic. And much more in order than with Samris kingdom's regular Arcana Corps...

So already a couple days later the elves entered the capital, where they got the king's head from one of the previously-gifted elf-maidens. To the king's credit must note that the second concubine to him so warmed in this time that preferred to die together with her royal master. Subsequently this became the basis for classical play of the first woman-playwright Villina Herzstadt "Aeriel and Tailon." Which to this day gathers abundant harvest of tears and snots in all more-or-less civilized theaters of Alkirke.

Further events in Samris developed quite predictably. Already a month after described events elves turned into the kingdom's aristocracy, displacing previous aristocracy to level at best of bourgeois. And turned out they had all this time their own royal couple. Namely Enissar and Doriel the Beautiful — yes-yes, those very ones whose sacred names for every elf were mentioned by Quarandil in the reception hall of duke Ariette. Actually the title "Conqueror" Enissar got precisely after Samris operation. Well and Doriel her bore from childhood and also quite deservedly. By the moment when nearest neighbors of the kingdom roused themselves to help (which the unfortunate Tailon due to suddenness of attack didn't manage to ask for) — Samris was officially renamed Concordia Danaan.

Pilgrimages of ambassadors of nearest states — graciously received the elves. Politely assuring the slightly stunned ambassadors that all trade agreements remained in force. With which they were dismissed. Characteristically, the new government fulfilled said obligations. As result — the unexpected coup in the formerly quite ordinary and unremarkable kingdom caused no special panic in neighbors. In vain, of course.

Further everything developed swiftly. The elves, having settled in their new kingdom — suddenly began multiplying. And so that surrounding eyes climbed onto foreheads. On average an elf-maiden at one time bore from four to six children. In separate cases up to eight. Less than two — never. For this worked everything — and excellent health characteristic apparently of all representatives of the race, and incredible plasticity of bodies of young (and not very) elf-maidens, and their lush hips, and especially breasts which could feed a legion. Pregnancy, by the way, lasted only five months. And subsequent development of newborn elflings correspondingly also went at accelerated rates. True, turned out that lifespan of elves also was very short. On average by fifty years already very deep old age set in, longer than sixty held no one. Exception was only the royal couple, but this surprising fact related already to their relationship with their gods, which judging by everything completely changed metabolism characteristic of all other elves.

Brevity of life of long-eared inhabitants of Danaan was compensated by completely unreal rates of maturation. Actually, sexual maturity, as turned out, elves reach already by age six-seven. And since training began from swaddling — then intellectual and social maturity came approximately then too. The peak of all abilities came at ten years and didn't fade until end of days, becoming on slope of years just a bit less intense. Thus, already seven-eight years after appearance of next "batch" of children on light — elves had new, trained, with excellent base preparation warriors — and new mothers for upcoming fresh replenishments.

But here entered into play another feature of this fertile tribe. Almost two thirds of newborns turned out boys. And deficit elf-maidens far not all turned out fertile. Actually, multiply and reproduce could only one of four. But — until very death, since elves in principle did not know what old age was. Simply closer to death they became ever less active, slept ever more, and in the end painlessly "switched off" like broken dolls. Externally they continued at this sad moment to look as fresh and beautiful as in their 10 years.

"Fruitful" representatives of fair sex as a rule dedicated life to continuous bearing and birthing children. The rest — were just as feminine and seductive beauties, at that — magnificent warriors or professionals in other areas, as well as cherished reward for most deserving elves. Yes, earning right to multiply and to spouse from among non-fertile elf-maidens managed only the very best of them. Which naturally went only to elven phenotype's benefit. Distinguished by special valor or achieving great successes in his field elf entered into legal marriage with his barren chosen. Five months later he received in hands his children from one of representatives of Mor-matar-ta — caste of Great Mothers, to which all fruitful elf-maidens from five years belonged. Further children were raised (and what's interesting, breast-fed!) by spouse of the premium-receiving lucky one, while biological mother of his offspring — was already bearing next batch of children from next hero.

Elven society took shape as caste, and besides Mor-matar-ta (the only, by the way, caste, enrollment into which happened automatically by fact of ability to fertilize) existed ten more castes, each corresponding to its professional designation. Need to remind, from birth and until five years all elves were raised as future warriors; alongside developed and other inherent from birth talents and abilities, well and inherent to every representative of long-eared tribe fine sense of beautiful didn't need developing, it was present initially. At five-year age elves took their first exam, by results of which preliminary distribution happened. At ten years already fully adult elf passed through final, second exam, making preliminary distribution final — or ending with transfer to another, more suitable caste.

The first and most numerous was Laok-ta — caste of warriors and other "force-types," to which by birthright belonged every elf. At five years of age, if future warrior discovered clear abilities (or demonstrated absence of abilities required for other castes) — he passed first initiation and replenished ranks of combat, undergoing special preparation and distinguished by special mentality elves. Thus, besides technical combat qualities — these girls and guys were distinguished by considering themselves already initially dead. Which substantially eased any task on battlefield: elf-warriors in principle felt no piety before death.

For not-passing-selection to Laok-ta and to other castes remained not so honorable but extremely needed both in peacetime and wartime Ayril-ta. These elves were builders, craftsmen, smiths, farmers... True, in most cases elves even from this caste preferred fine work where they showed rare mastery and excellent artistic taste. As for all heavy and dirty work — since renaming of Samris into Concordia exclusively people engaged in it. Of course — under sensitive guidance and supervision of elves from Ayril-ta.

Perhaps the most atypical caste was Cian-ta. This was community of young rebels and researchers, travelers and poets. They differed from others by inquisitiveness, sociability and far broader view of the world. This bunch of legalized nonconformists somewhat balanced extremely strict, closed and hierarchical elf world. Moreover — representatives of Cian-ta tribe often smoothly transferred to another caste. And it was the smallest Tiarn-ta. Caste of leaders.

Fifth in counting was Sail-ta — caste of sages and scientists. Here got elves with strongly expressed humanitarian or at least partly technical thinking. Philosophers, doctors, historians, teachers — here were all who worked with brains much more than with hands and feet. For completely atypical, but still by luck found, even rarely, among elves minds — existed Phugi-ta, uniting financiers, treasurers and other virtuosos of accounting art — in word, all those who were capable of finding beauty and harmony even in dry world of figures.

Krai-ta became those elves whose nature lay in sphere of art. Artists, singers, musicians, dancers — strictly speaking, these skills to significant degree all in whose veins flowed at least drop of elven blood possessed — but here gathered really the best. By way, heralds most often were recruited precisely among them.

Elven religion remained for people mystery. But to gods (or god) they obviously served, and for this important sphere answered representatives of Seidge-ta caste. Note that whatever the elven pantheon was — on calls of its followers it obviously responded, so long-eared clerics both in peace and in war were extremely needed.

But all power of clerics on battlefield paled in comparison with possibilities of Ruin-ta — great mages of Danaan. As soon got to verify the carefree neighboring kingdoms — art of elven mages didn't yield to human, and main — there were many of them, which ensured to ordinary troops of elves not bad fire support.

Last and most mysterious caste were Spair-ta. Members of this caste became employees of elven secret police, diplomatic corps and other no less notable establishments. Actually — big details about this caste no one had. As already said — second exam, at ten years, finally fixed elf in his caste. Further he went his path until his very death.

Separately need say about royal couple. Rulers of long-eared tribe were distinguished by such enviable longevity that contemporaries of overthrown Tailon, as well as several subsequent human generations had all grounds to consider them and outright immortals. However, as history showed, aging and death of rulers were still inevitable — but only set in on third century of their lives and only after birth of single (in this they also differed from all) heir. In this case — heiress, which was Runiel the Wise. Contemporary of duke Ariette...

Concordia Danaan expanded. Practically continuously. Next captured areas became empire Hornvaald in east and caliphate Az-Akhturkh in south. Many-year war allowed elves to dump internal demographic pressure, perfect army, and at same time seriously expanded their territories. So seriously that new neighbors — rashly considered themselves safe, and remained in this blissful certainty several quickly-flashing decades... After which sequentially familiarized themselves first with representatives of Laok-ta and alas, Ruin-ta — and then went into direct disposition of Ayril-ta.

As result by moment when people understood-after-all that need to assemble alliances — Concordia grew to very impressive sizes, occupying almost all coast of Red Ocean and quite substantially deepening into continent. By size and population current Concordia Danaan exceeded Golden Dol at least two to two-and-a-half times.

In other conditions — could generally say that duchy lost war before first battle. In other. But duchy had its trumps up sleeve too.
