Chapter 8

Mazari attentively looked at delivered specimen. Him she left "for dessert," after all these dogs, sentenced, girl whom Antea for some reason needed...

Nooo... Orc — always requires thoughtful approach.

Right before sorceress, held by two assisting biomats stood at attention magnificent specimen of orc.

Generally speaking, capturing alive and undamaged hardened orc rarely succeeds. As rule — this turns out consequence of error made by orc. And these guys err not often.

Actually, standing before Mazari — erred only in one. He and his friend chose as Child Tulius Maximus himself, general of "Ducal Maniple." Being leader of special purpose unit included in main command reserve, Tulius was obviously one of few people capable of defeating average orc in one-on-one combat or holding against two long enough for his personal guard fighters to run up. However — woman managed to escape. Pity but even vivisection of male — allowed experienced biomage to comprehend much.

Unfortunately, orcs even into Mazari's lab fell extremely rarely. Cause of this were very strict laws established throughout civilized world regarding these red-skinned. Due to their reproduction peculiarities accompanied as rule by Pilgrimage — had to observe some delicacy.

Mother of Monsters approached prisoner. Obeying her mental orders, one of biomats tore remnants of clothing from orc.

As Mazari supposed, orc was hardened. On body remained even no atavistic traces of transformation. Hair covering was developed, smoothly transitioning from huge lush white mane on head to sacrum along spine, narrowing down. Height was almost one-and-a-half average-human.

Under skin of brick color — bulged muscles. Overall — orc was built quite proportionally and if Mazari's assistants were ordinary people not ideally-designed biomats lovingly sculpted by scientist from sentenced — he probably long would have freed himself. Actually even now, judging by rippling muscles — he periodically tried to break free.

Mazari deeply inhaled, once more formulated in head source code of spell which was to structure and direct flow of raw mana, after which with smooth exhale freed construction and filled it with life. As always when dealing with orc — spell simply got absorbed into flesh of red-skinned giant, merely making him displeased frown.

Orcs strictly speaking real historical race were not. They didn't create this world like ancestors of people, didn't arise in subterranean depths like first-dwarves. They even didn't come from Beyond Edge like elves. Actually after elves youngest people were they.

By their origin they owed to magic catastrophe that destroyed flourishing Allia which once occupied position of superpower of this continent and left as heritage to descendants Great Pummikan Desert.

Strictly speaking, before this catastrophe, orcs — were people. In general — they made up population of that very Allia. This was completely ordinary people, not differing in anything from their neighbors... Well except believed they not in Pantheon of Light like overwhelming majority of people inhabiting continent Manor, but in own god — Azarius. Now hard to say how it happened, and not so this important. Fact is that Azarius to prayers of his adherents quite responded, was considered god more than light, and even moderately venerated outside borders of state, though as rule there were chapels at embassies and consulates of state.

On that memorable day when Pummikan decided to experiment with forces obviously exceeding human reason, Azarius participated in next battle of endless war of Darkness and Light. Glory to Creator that by laws established by him, all skirmishes of this war directly on face of Alkirke — reflected in no way, except for intrigues into which were drawn mortals. Nevertheless — clashes between immortal representatives of these camps happened regularly and quite some, and in one of them shoulder to shoulder with Pantheon of Light — fought also Azarius. Not that this brought any noticeable profit, but — need somehow shorten eternity, right?

In general, that on place of flourishing country remained soaked-with-magic-radiation ruins in which alive remained at best one person in thousand, who turned out most resistant to magic, and even so — at present moment survivors mutate into who-knows-what, but who as can — Azarius learned somewhat later than should.

Of course — he as could quickly moved to ruins of his home, already on way feeling how his force previously fed by crowds of believers — swiftly slips through fingers. Dark god, in this place, would simply try to concentrate remnants of energy, and go into hibernation trying to last as long as possible. Azarius was light, and at that from radically-light. So seeing torments and sufferings of pathetic remnants of his people — he threw all available power of faith — into transformation of survivors into form which would survive happened catastrophe.

Actually — so appeared first orcs. Of course, way Azarius applied was rough, unnatural and close to forbidden techniques. He simply inseparably tied bodies and souls of his surviving followers. Souls and bodies merged into single conglomerate — transformed. Soul got possibility to be fed by same as body. Body — got all might of soul. However — this gave unexpected effects.

Firstly — future orcs lost souls, and possibly even afterlife.

Secondly — they fundamentally changed. Soul having become one with flesh — transformed flesh. First of all — orcs became fundamentally insusceptible to direct effect of magic. Average orc ignored direct hit of fireball (though — house lit on fire from hit of this very ball, collapsing on orc — would kill this orc), didn't give damn about magic lightning, but equally — was insusceptible also to effect of healing spells for example. However — in healing orcs and didn't need. Point is their new soul-soaked flesh — was capable of closing literally before eyes. Tenacity orcs acquired simply legendary. Wounds that could kill any other living being — orcs bore either fully on feet or in brief coma. Reliably kill orc could only by taking off head or chopping to really small pieces. Cut-off limbs — orcs quickly restored. Blood loss didn't threaten them. Instead of blood their veins were filled with some silver ichor which by way had huge value — it healed wounds in any living beings with incredible speed. Actually — assumed this was orcish soul. Need note ichor on "cut" was released literally in quantity of pair of drops only stimulating healing, so "milking" orc — also didn't work, and more than pair of drops of ichor — orc could give only voluntarily.

Besides — orcs turned out completely insusceptible to all diseases. Apparently told that metabolism became completely magical. Or theological — if take into account that fault of all was blessing of their own god. Note that funny feature of such metabolism became almost reduced excretory system. Orcs absorbed everything ate by 99%. As result with one hand — extremely rarely needed food, and with other — not so often visited bathrooms. However incomprehensibly from what but this very process for them became sacred, requiring prayer before its start and ritual of cleansing — after. This wouldn't deserve special attention of researchers if not for fact that orcs at least officially to their god Azarius related extremely cold. So surrounding act of body ridding of some excesses with mystic tinsel apparently — was just another way to morally kick their heavenly patron.

Actually relations of orcs with their god were built on representation of both sides about huge fault of Azarius. While he "played at war" — in his country, his people who faithfully and diligently worshipped him and glorified his name — ended up in trouble. And was almost completely exterminated. Those who saved themselves — in end stopped being people.

Fact that transformation came out rather positive and considering all positive qualities of orcs including such small bonus as biological immortality caused considerable envy in many people — formally not taken into account. Speaking not formally — orcs long considered themselves higher race, treating others with quite noticeable haughtiness. Actually — possibly they would long try to conquer surrounding world, but just there were few of them. Well and besides — mind in them changed following body, so thought of conquering world came into orcish heads only as flat and outdated joke — really: why to immortal, almost invulnerable, higher beings such bother???

Externally orcs likewise ceased to be people, becoming effectively new species. First of all — sexual dimorphism aggravated, becoming even sharper than in people.

Orcs-males — were tall (order of two and half meters in height) athletically built humanoids, with perhaps even excessively beautiful faces resembling rather faces of sculptures. At that beauty was not feminine but quite masculine: aquiline profile, well-cut cheekbones — on Earth average orc would probably be taken for extremely idealized depiction of Indian chief of some Delaware or Chinook. Overall, orcs differed in extremely developed musculature and massiveness, but at that — unlike over-pumped representatives of human tribe didn't lose grace and precision of movements. Practically whole body of orcs was completely deprived of hair covering except brows and huge and lush mane of absolutely white hair going from head down neck and effectively to very sacrum, gradually narrowing. This feature need note was characteristic for both sexes. Skin of males had reddish-brown shade.

Females were somewhat smaller in height (as rule within two-with-small meters), built somewhat less massively and much less muscularly (in their case more correct would be speak of figures of track-and-field athletes), but at that — femininity of build was possibly even excessive. Thus always possessing "wasp" waist, orcs-females were proud bearers of disproportionately huge breast and quite wide hips with prominent buttocks. However in combination with general athleticism of their build and invariably long and slender legs — this looked even attractively, though extremely exotically. Females of orcs were distinguished even greater gracefulness than males. Skin covering of females — also differed from coloring of males, having rather color of strong coffee with milk.

In principle, as people, orcs turned out quite adequate beings. With all their mental changes they didn't lose enough humanity to have ability to communicate with other people. Absence in them of conquering ambitions — made them seemingly quite cute neighbors (however should understand general peaceful disposition of orcs was dictated not by their weakness but only by huge value of every orc's life — after all afterlife for them didn't exist, so live they had to "here and now." At that, due to monstrous tenacity, endurance, strength and animal grace and coordination — warriors they were most magnificent).

Orcs built themselves beautiful city practically in very center of Pummikan Desert which was once their homeland (not many were people who risked visit this place since echoes of magic catastrophe made being-of-living-people in heart of magic desert matter of considerable danger). Orcs were magnificent masters and creators creating magnificent works of art from statuettes to weapons (in personal collection of king Artex was full armor and sword work of orcish smiths which in his view were highlight of collection... And accompanyingly cost as half of rest of collection).

And there would be no problems with them... if not for their reproduction....

Point is being "soulless" beings — orcs were completely deprived of possibility of continuing race. No, orcs could love and loved (amusing fact but with orcs in principle didn't occur sexual perversions. Apparently due to perfection). Orcs created families....

But to bring forth new life — need soul. To create soul — two human beings must each add piece from their soul. And how to take piece from what isn't there?

And then — orcs began stealing lives.

Yes, orcs couldn't conceive new life. Yes, orc female couldn't carry. But — they wanted children. And was concluded pact with their god.

Actually — essence turned out very simple. To bring forth new orc — required human. Orcs in principle chose adult, established-in-life representative of human tribe. Selection criteria were incomprehensible to anyone. Choice most often fell on ordinary townsfolk or peasant, but could fall on dirty beggar and on famed military leader and on princess.

Be that as it may, but future "child" was kidnapped. And taken to heart of desert...

What happened further in black Tents of Birth Priests — knew only orcish god. Even Birth Priests themselves — never entered Tent from moment of sunset to moment of sunrise. Indisputable truth was only one — taken into tent, drugged with special infusion of sacred herbs of Pummikan Desert chosen — changed. Judging by sounds coming from tent all long night — changes were excruciating. Luckily — by morning he no longer remembered anything that happened. All he remembered was that before sunset — he was brought into this tent as human. And taken out after sunrise — as orc. However — if we say he then taken out — her. Yes, all correct. Human not only changed. Also his sex changed. Instead of human woman — appeared on light orc-male. Instead of man — female. Horror of situation also lay in that "newborn" remembered perfectly who he was and who he was before rebirth. Remembered all who awaited him, who loved and whom he loved himself. But... Could already do nothing with this. Turn orc back — managed yet no one. Actually reverse transformation was grail of all biomages of world. Unattainable but desired.

On exit from Tent — orc was met by "parents." To his special amazement — he not only didn't feel hatred to them but on contrary — was grateful to them to depth of soul.... And besides — felt coming from very depth of heart love. Also completely sincere. This — was part of monstrous transformation. But was also one more part. Now with parents him bound eternal Bonds. Child — simply COULDN'T not obey his "parents." Moreover — similar Bonds — bound him also with rest of Family. With brothers and sisters — likewise in time kidnapped by "parents." No, them Child didn't obey. But — felt kinship and love. Important understand these feelings didn't seem to converted unnatural. This went from depth of henceforth-absent in him soul. By way — brothers they weren't just so. All Children of one Family — were similar. To each other and obviously also to Parents. To same measure — in which would resemble if born natural way. Of course, having met new family, Child nevertheless went into Pilgrimage. So orcs called parting with their human nature. Parting beginning really with long walk to his old home...

Think not hard to imagine how former neighbors and friends met — changed person. Most often — sufficed quite small amount of time for newborn orc to stop being human. Orc he didn't yet become but with people no longer identified self. In this period — he traveled human kingdoms searching self. Even though in most kingdoms touching orc outside frames of self-defense was forbidden. Nevertheless — also were those who returning met understanding. Those whom welcomed and gladly met at home. Them were quite few, units... But they were. Orcs related to this with understanding. After all time anyway played in their favor. Sooner or later — nature won. Orc more and more noticed that he faster, stronger, more beautiful and more tenacious than people. Sees more, hears more, knows more. To him cramped and stuffy among people. Sooner or later — he distanced. Distancing becoming more and more alien. Becoming already finally orc. In term from year to ten years — orc "matured." In this same time — he finally changed externally. If young orc not too strongly differed from ordinary human, then by moment of return to Pummikan Desert — he finally became same as that one which at present moment stood in laboratory of Al Sharif.

Mazari set aside turned-out-useless separator and applied cut-off hand of orc back to wound. As she expected — ichor from both ends of cuts stretched finest silver cobwebs, connected and as soon as hand turned out correctly aligned — fused them. Orc though almost imperceptibly but with relief exhaled. Remove ichor from amputation stump even using specially-developed-for-this-task separator — didn't work. Transformation potion — also worked refused. Neither swallowed (orc by way licked it with noticeable appetite, apparently got hungry during interrogations) nor introduced into vein (due to absence of vein of course). Spells — also turned out useless and even dangerous for Mazari herself. Thus, one of fire — destroyed biomat responsible for left hand. If not for prudently put in reserve biomat-gorilloid, Mazari might not live to evening. Luckily, bought by case at wandering circus elderly monkey who'd passed all circles of hell at Mother of Monsters — turned into best bodyguard imaginable. So — gorilloid without difficulty twisted orc and held him in such state until limped next biomat from warehouse.

Mazari pensively poked orc's bicep with pointer. Tissues were dense and elastic. No trace of age changes. Actually — this prompted her to next idea, but at this very moment in hallway sounded noise. Mazari mentally ordered gorilloid to pack orc into special pen (so named for ability to hold inside even wild steppe bull during rut), after which set aside paper and with flying gait headed to sort out disturbers of peace. Soul from realization of own idea's brilliance simply sang... At least until Mazari threw open doors...
