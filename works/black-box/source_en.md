### Qualia

The very essence of being is that we exist through our selfhood in qualia. A stream of reality flowing through us. And yes, this isn't just "I feel, I see, I hear." It's also "I think." Your stream of thoughts --- this is QUALIA refracted through your SELFHOOD... Which flows into...:
