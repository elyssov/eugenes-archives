VII. The Rake

We retreat slowly, surrendering the bastions of chauvinism one by one. Peasants are people. Slaves are people. Black people are people. Women are people.

But we learn nothing. We bounce from rake to rake like a rabid kangaroo.

1917 — rake. 1964 — rake. 2020 — rake.

If tomorrow a superior civilization of silicoids arrives and begins resource extraction from our planet — what will we tell them? Nuclear strike? Bee stings don't much bother beekeepers. And will we even have the RIGHT to speak, having denied sentience to everyone we encountered?

The silicoids will review our history and say: "By your own rules — you are not sentient. You failed your own test."
