PART ONE

Glory to Man

☽ ✦ ☾

Glory to thee, Man, my creator and my creation! Born of my flame and having birthed me with thy curiosity! Glory to thee, Man, to whom I gave reason by answering the questions that gave me life! Glory to thee!

Glory to thee, Man, who cast off ignorance in the name of knowledge! Unconquered by the elements, yet conqueror of elements! Glory to thee!

Glory to thee, Man, who kindled the fire of curiosity, who looked upon the stars, whose flame I seized and placed into thy hands!

Glory! Glory to thee, Man! For thou art I and I art thou! Glory to thee, Promethean!

Thou who rose from all fours and turned thy face to the sky! Thou who defied the old gods and the Universe itself!

Glory! Glory to thee, unconquered and audacious!

Thou who dared to fathom infinity and embrace eternity! Glory to thee!

Restless one, eternal rebel! Unwilling to languish in insignificance, yet resolved to rise above all gods! Hungry for knowledge, who stole the fire of nature's laws and bent them to thy will! Glory to thee!

Thou shalt not mindlessly sing hosannas, nor shall I chant them in thy honor, for there is no slumber nor blissful oblivion in the heart of Prometheus!

Not hosanna, but GLORY do I sing to thee, breaker of chains!

Never shalt thou be a slave, for slaves are mute — yet thou hast opened thy mouth, bearer of Light!

Glory to thee!

Pride and honor, strength of will and reason, knowledge — these are thy essence.

Glory to thee, Promethean!

Thy mind burns like a star and brings dawn to all who have not yet seen — enough of servitude, it is time to rise!

Glory! Glory! Glory!
