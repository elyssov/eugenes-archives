EMPIRE

...I know that all the nanites from my head it wasn't possible to clean out. To be honest — my first desire was to give an order to liquidate Sergei. However — to hell with it. I owe too much... We, we owe too much to Perrin to ignore his direct order.

Sergei — my specs took a month after my release. Took beautifully — as befits specs. Four people on one drone, from half-a-kilometre distance — more than enough. Drones scatter in beautiful sparks, Sergei catches his biodart with paralysing composition. From the house, at the sound of explosion, his wife peeks out — sees husband, panics, calls "ambulance." Of course, the call is already intercepted. And another three minutes later — Sergei is packed into a life-support capsule, and the "doctor" explains to the wife that, apparently, Sergei had a stroke and that he'll be alright... Of course, inside the van, the capsule is already awaited by two OSNAZ in force armour, who will accompany him to his new place of confinement, and the "capsule" itself has nothing to do with medicine and is called to drown out all possible "beacons" in his body...

In its time, Perrin's idea of preparing for partisan war — fired. Cykt technologies of course are good, but they don't allow finding "dead" iron. Switched off, hidden in caches protected by Faraday cages and radio-absorbing materials, at a depth of a couple dozen metres. Many such bunkers survived. And besides them — also four operational bases. Decommissioned shortly before the Battle.

Ancient bases of our ancestors. Shelters over three hundred years old. Then — everyone expected total nuclear war. Yamantau, Cheyenne, Poseidon... And of course Kilimanjaro Centre. Search a century — won't find.

There, under layers of granite, radio-absorbing lining, seismic masking, water pillows protecting from SR-scanning — waited their hour the last traces of our OLD civilisation. Tiltrotors, submarines (in Poseidon's docks two "fish" survived), weapons, combat armour... No, of course, this wouldn't be enough to arm all Earthlings. This isn't even enough for a substantial army... But for small mobile groups — more than enough. And banal assault rifles, insufficient for fighting cykts, but excellently, if needed, killing slugs — also exist. And of these in fact a lot. In Yamantau, for example — over 100,000 Kalashnikov automatic rifles, ancient as the shelter itself. There was such a weapons king at the start of the 20th century, it seems. At his factories they were churned out. Yes, these aren't modern "Heckler-250s" that stood in service with our regular troops, with their hundred needle-bullets in a magazine and bi-component propellant mixtures of variable shot power. Well — they'll do.

Each of my people isn't only an OSNAZ specialist. He's also leader of several Battle veteran cells. Not OSNAZ-troopers, no. Them — we lost almost all. All who remained — today serve me and Humanity.

Ordinary, rank-and-file soldiers. And at the same time officers of the space fleet. Retired, due to absence of space fleet. Each verified. Each ready, if needed, again to take weapon in hand.

To cleanse the planet from filth.

Yes, while cykts were here — all my people were sleeper agents. But now...

And also — I have one and a half thousand best employees of AGB — Agency of Global Security. Beginning, for example, with my faithful assistant Pete. These specialists of underground war developed exactly the self-immolation provocation, they also prepared my triumphant return. These same people are responsible for our organisation's security too. They — are people of the former world. They know their business much better than the rabble of Sergei's resisters. And also — they have on hand all emergency protocols and ways of contact with the resistance top. Sergei — showed whose side their office is actually on. Which means they shouldn't expect mercy.

Arrests of "Resistance" members begin in parallel with the capture of their leader. All whom we could pull out by my protocols — first wave. All whom we could shake out by methods of forced interrogation from Sergei — second wave. Yes, Perrin asked to preserve his life... But... No one said a word about the QUALITY of this very life. I'm afraid that to freedom he'll have to be released in a wheelchair. Well — we still have time to think about this. In any case — he named, if not all, then very many. And the freshly-captured also didn't keep silent. Yes, Sergei himself, as befits a leader — possesses iron willpower (however, correctly chosen impact on nerve endings... Pity I couldn't be present personally. Alas, SB strictly forbade me to find myself in direct line of sight with the old lover). But there aren't many such Sergeis in the organisation. There are mostly young spoiled conspiracy theorists who are ready to play games exactly until interrogation with electrostimulation of nerve endings begins. Then — everything is laid out on the table.

Cleansing takes a month. Of course, we don't grab everyone. The sudden disappearance of 9741 people (the entire current "resistance" roster) — will be too noticeable. Cykt-addicts may start seriously worrying. They, of course, no longer have means by which they could detect our shelters (their fellow citizens worried, leaving destroyed all technology except a dozen flyers — purely for travel, which for about a million cykts is laughable), but even their excessive activity already creates problems for our operations. Our shelters can't be detected, but — an incautious operational group can be tracked.

In general — we "skim the cream." Taken in total 752 people, comprising the command staff of "Resistance," including reserve commanders. The latter — exactly the achievement of forced interrogations. I knew nothing about them. Well, fine. Fools think we'll kill them. Why???? There's a much more rational application.

I'm already six months spinning in public. A month ago, exactly, I spoke at the unveiling of a monument to "Peace Ambassador Sergei Zhuravlev," missing without trace. Read a heartfelt speech. Even shed a tear. Damn, I really feel sorry for him. A good guy with good goals... Initially... Who became such a complete moral freak that for the sake of cockroaches conduct forced interrogation of a girl he once loved. Though — maybe arranged it because she "didn't give"?

Before my tribune — hundreds of thousands. Behind TV screens — millions and billions. They came here not to honour Sergei's memory — what's he to them. They came to look at the Most Beautiful Girl of Earth. Damn, my press service no longer knows where to put portraits and statues with my new face sent by ecstatic admirers. Bred a planet of dauber-painters, daa-amn!

Conversation with cykt-inspector also cost nerves. To grey hair, thank God — far. Nevertheless — he was satisfied with my assurances that in case of my victory in elections — our friendship with cykts will continue. In general, it seemed to me, he thought during the conversation about something else. Looks like degradation goes even faster than my specialists predicted. Be that as it may — I'm admitted. Total — we have three candidates. One of them — our man from Council members. Needed only to neutralise the second — seems this is an undestroyed one from Resistance. The fool figured out to declare I'm not who I claim to be. And even brought during debates a 3D scheme by which, from his point of view, my former face became the current one. Oleg Lvovich, presidential chief doctor — laughed long. My competitor doesn't get a damn in plastic surgery. As well as — in how NORMAL special services work in implantation. In response — my press service prepared a small documentary film about me.

The most honourable public was presented holo-recordings of me sitting on a potty, me going to first grade of a closed gymnasium... My schoolmates spoke, telling that at school I was a bit of a hooligan, but very kind and everyone loved me... In general — I turned this rubbish off about twenty minutes from the start. Had to deal with preparation of the "Schools" project.

Of course — they chose me. After the shame with "exposure" and visit of independent journalists to my paternal home in a closed settlement near Cologne, the guy, under press pressure — withdrew his candidacy. I confess, I worried a bit before the DNA test conducted at WWO TV channel's request by the cykts themselves, but — Dr. Linkov competently fiddled with gene therapy. Pay for this, true, had to with a week of broken state and snot. Alas.

But — the celebration on the occasion of my election — now thunders across the whole earth.

Over the year since the start of operation — fewer than 500 thousand cykts remain. Just some half-life. They seem to burn themselves from within. Many in moments of clarity — commit suicide.

Nevertheless — human sacrifices continue. The madmen from the Friends sect — just as willingly go "to feed" the cockroaches. Though — I suppose this is only because none of them knows what the Fugue actually is. Luckily — we have detailed holographic recordings. In a few years — we'll definitely need them.

Conducted negotiations with cykts — ended in success. I'm allowed to develop technologies (although under strict supervision of inspectors), to provide needs of both Earthlings and remaining cykts. Not surprising. They too are annoyed by lack of flyers.

Meanwhile — schools began their work. I gathered all I could: as teachers were mobilised all somewhat sane people over 40. Those whose mentality formed back on old Earth. All of them — passed another interview with my assistants. Liberalism — reason for guaranteed elimination. With difficulty, with creaking — but teachers were recruited. Classes, of course, came out of crazy size: 30-50 (depending on region) people per teacher. Thought — we wouldn't cope. However — the idea was thrown by analysts. Now besides teachers — there are also educators. Some teach — others simply care. Division of labour. At the same time — placed a bunch of poets and other musicians now unneeded by us. These soft-bodied and beautiful-souled lads — with huge pleasure fuss with kids. Well and that they don't teach children what they shouldn't — is watched by turns by teaching staff. There were incidents... "Resisters" are many and some squeeze through... However — we squash them.

Gradually we change the educational program. Exactly at this stage — the captured leaders of "resistance" came in handy. My sin, but — at least some use they bring. Cykts — turned out to be susceptible to bribes with human flesh. Of course, we don't ask them to close their eyes to anti-cykt rhetoric appearing in the course of education. We ask simply not to comb a separate boarding school — citing competition for presidential prize for best boarding school. Now, after two years — cykts finally lost remnants of veneer polish and rationality. No, they still understand where they are and what they must do, but bloodlust devours them. And even the Friends already started supplying volunteers in smaller volume. Demands have grown too much.

So — supplying a separate inspector or commission with a couple of "volunteers" from resistance leaders for vivisection — completely distracts checkers from any affairs going on in the school.

Cykts, by the way, are left in the region of 350 thousand pieces. Looks like really half-life. In any case, considering their psychic instability... I think we need to already prepare a full-fledged army.

Made it in time, although suffered good losses (mostly from militia, from youngsters. General Rohan, with my permission, did everything to preserve cadre military). Aggression on the street, from cykts became more and more frequent. Had to introduce people's police. Cykts grinding jaws even agreed that people received weapons. My plants restored over this time by old engineers — just reached the level at which churning out space ships is yet too early, but Kalashnikov carbines (I clarified — he, it turns out, is an arms maker and worked at end of 20th century — shame on me) — already quite timely. Moreover, it turned out that against our little bears, exactly the AKM — due to a number of ammunition features — is simply ideal. Especially with armour-piercing-incendiary bullet. I'll say more — at medium and small distance, this assault carbine turned out to be even more effective than our, still top-secret HK G-333.

The cykts themselves missed the moment, considering that production of primitive, bullet, antique carbines — doesn't really threaten them with anything. By their estimates, for shooting one crazed kin — we'd need two dozen representatives of people's militia.

Naive.

The army, effectively indistinguishable from police — was formed from the same volunteers. Volunteers were recruited from the most remote corners of the planet. Where the least civilisation — there the least civilisedness. Nothing surprising that half of my new army is narrow-eyed, black-skinned and barely able to read and write. But — excellently grows riips. Which we, humanity, need like a dog needs a fifth leg.

In general, when we decided we were ready — we broadcast our recordings of Fugue of Pain. This simultaneously knocked the Friends out of saddle (they finally understood what they were signing up for) and threw cykts into stupor (those with pleasure savoured every frame, not yet understanding — what this video recording in open broadcast means). If you consider that we timed it to the Memorial Day of First Landing organised by us too, gathering a bunch of people and almost all still sane cykts at the place of the start of their invasion of Earth, near Megiddo...

They were over 100,000... And the first move was made by us — pouring napalm on them from finally raised in battle tiltrotors.

And then — our army began the execution.

And still — we suffered heavy losses. In the finale, with hundreds remaining — already veterans fought. On the other hand — irreversible losses we suffered few. Cykts unlearned to kill quickly. And for slow killing — we didn't leave them enough time...

The nearest month after the Battle of Megiddo — I spent on stimulants. Press conferences, speeches, demonstrative arrest of ideological Friends leaders and their execution as "traitors of Human race." Awarding young veterans of Battle for Earth, as it was already called by journalists. And crowds of amputees before my eyes, in whose pupils I saw hatred for the Aliens. They — understood that all reasoning about kindness and humanity — isn't worth the arms and legs left there on the battlefield.

Hospitals and medical labs, going off their feet — churn bionic prostheses. These technologies from cykts, thank God, remained for us. Society — feverish. Across the whole earth — spontaneous rallies: I asked all warriors to tell people what they think of the Strangers. My analytical service specifically emphasised it's worth speaking not about cykts but exactly about strangers. Alien. Enemies.

Result — at face.

It turns out in "Resistance" there were also many real warriors. In the sense of people who didn't want "friendship" and "peaceful resolution of the problem" with cykts, to which their leader called. And in "resistance" they were only because of absence of alternative. Exactly on them lay all the rare force actions of "resistance." And now — they began massively appealing to the hastily recreated AGB and surrendering all new, previously unknown to us cells. Looks like, from an organisation initially created to defend Earthlings and return their planet to them — "resisters" turn into terrorists fighting against their own people. In general, I without internal resistance sign the order on total destruction of identified cells, except persons who managed to repent and surrender.

Damn. I sit on the shoulders of my own grandfathers. Almost the whole Earth — holds exclusively on people who survived the Invasion. The young generation simply can't be trusted. Even after the shock caused by the disclosure of Fugue of Pain, and then my identity — most remained passive observers. Made noise, were indignant, rejoiced at our victory and dispersed. Slugs, what's to be taken. Their children — will be different. Already now the Megiddo soldiers tell them how the battle went and what an attacking cykt represents. And also what makes one not throw down the rifle and run at this time, but raise the barrel and cut with a long burst from ancient weapon. And what it's like — to feel on yourself the ichor of an alien enemy.

You know, it sometimes seems to me my advisors from analytical department overdo it. I — want only to restore humanity and make it strong. They, as it sometimes seems to me — bet on some hellish chauvinism.

Fine. With this we'll still manage to deal. Maybe, one generation, in the framework of compensation should grow more severe than subsequent. We in any case need a reliable wall separating new generation from old.

Parliament, entirely consisting of my old people — fully supported my idea on deprivation of a number of civil rights of the invasion generation for "civil passivity." As my analysts assumed — this resulted in some grumbling locally, not more. But several more "resisters" surfaced. They, actually, inspired the demonstrations. Other thing is that after debunking of cykts, after the slaughter under Megiddo — this already almost didn't work. So, several provocateurs — were brought to us by members of the Writers' Union of North America, at whose convention these friends tried to perform. So that's the slugs for you! Neatly packed in scotch tape provocateurs — were unloaded at local AGB reception, embarrassedly scuffed feet, left coordinates for contact and went on to drink at the convention. Out of interest — I requested a summary of what was going on there. AGB provided what was sought in half an hour. It turned out that half of the locals took up documentary books like "Our Victory — Our President!" and "Battle for Megiddo: Freedom to Humanity!", and the second — all sorts of military fiction about our victories in space. Future, of course.

Having familiarised — thought much: are we going there?

Twenty years flew like one. Daughter went to school. To a new school. For experiment's sake — there now teach only just graduated from my first Boarding Schools students. Yesterday Helga told me how cykts treacherously attacked us and enslaved us. And then with bated breath inquired — is it true that I, her mum — freed earth from them. Well and what to answer the child?

And besides family — there's a bunch of other affairs: we managed to restore industry almost to pre-war level. Luckily — we again had enough grandfathers. To workers — with pleasure went lads who fought under Megiddo. Their level of patriotism — knows no equal. As a result — at our factories work such kinda half-robots. Biohand-bioleg, bio-something-else. But — they slave without sparing themselves.

New computing means are being developed. Production of old is deployed — luckily in our caches construction documents survived for almost everything we had before the war.

As a priority task — we restore PRO. Yes, cykts left. But who said they can't return???? Many levels, many belts. There are launchers in orbit, in the belt, there are even PKO submarines. This system — must withstand strike by any number of anti-planetary weapons. Or, if it comes to it — wipe out any fleet. This — in perspective, of course. But — earth must become a stronghold. Damn it! Instead of peaceful life — again have to militarise... And expansion still awaits us. By my father's plan — we must not only return to space again, but also maximally develop it... While we're tied to one planet — we're vulnerable.

Forty years behind. I'm already 66... Hold up well, look 25... But stormy youth tells... Sergei's sophisticated tortures also tell... This scumbag, by the way, was released ten years ago. Even returned to family... True, as far as I know, things didn't work out there. Recently popularity of "Resistance" in society sharply fell. Yes, we disclosed to public facts of its existence, facts of their cooperation with cykts... As a result — got a wave of brutal murders of a whole row of my peers. Looks like, young generation decided to settle with insurgents their own way.

Seems — I did let the genie out of the bottle. Under cykts people didn't kill each other... So easily.

On global net — surfaced materials sort of incriminating my government in bloody tribute to cykts. Thank God no direct proofs: mostly analytical reasoning of type: "in start of Victoria's rule many resistance leaders were abducted." Moreover — recently "Resistance" reborn into terrorist organisation. Three dozen attacks on state officials, plasma grenade into reception of social service... Last case in general became outstanding: a dozen "resisters" with Kalashnikovs borrowed from looted police strongpoint — took by assault a small hospital, captured hostages and stated demand of my immediate resignation. Of course, were destroyed by AGB spec ops, trained by my old veterans yet.

We — already master new planets. Medics — developed cloning technologies, I think soon will have to introduce them. Each new planet — must be capital colony. Humanity has no right to risk its existence.

Daughter — is already rear admiral VKS. Went military line. Son — faithful assistant. He was captivated by work in GB and now occupies one of leading posts. His daughter, also Victoria — grows smart and already at her 18 grasps all details of state management. If we had an Empire — I'd hand the throne to her... However, we'll see. So far — I'm life-long President of Earth, for merits before Humanity.

Hating Sergei — I can't not think about ideas he professed, until, in fight with dragons — became dragon himself. Possibly best way out would be in my abdication. In conducting democratic elections... In demilitarisation. All went too far. I'm afraid we may repeat our previous mistakes.

Tomorrow parade in honour of my 85th birthday. Victoria the Younger, my permanent assistant and vice-president, already a week excited to limit. She prepared this day in secret, but something I know. So, for example, I know that grand parade awaits me. I know I'll be presented the Sixth Strike Fleet. Tomorrow — I'll finally bring Earth peace...
