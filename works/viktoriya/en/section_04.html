THE BEGINNING

The assault began when the man-made wormholes of hyperjumps closed behind the last ships of the "proper" cykts.

The Alcatraz guard was swept away by a synchronous salvo of H&K-333 railguns — in their time these rifles were developed precisely for the rapid destruction of cykts: the projectile, hitting the "little bears'" chitinous shell, guaranteed to break it, destructing inside and throwing into the cykt-trooper's body a huge number of striking elements in the form of depleted uranium darts. Unlike cykt weapons — killing quickly and without suffering — Earth rifles killed not at once, and wounded cykts could for another good half-hour rapturously envy the dead — immobilised, suffering from monstrous pain and guaranteed doomed.

Luckily for the cykts — this weapon entered service with the OSNAZ units "Phoenix" and "Narwhal." The regular Earth infantry didn't manage to be equipped with it, and most of the spec ops died during suicidal boarding attacks in the Battle for the Belt.

The remaining rifles the cykts methodically destroyed, the blueprints erased, and the designers run through the memory cleaning procedure. In general, the usually humane roaches — perceived this weapon as personal insult and treated it accordingly. They collected rifles literally piece by piece, checking against invoices. If not for the efforts of a small resistance group, the glorious and effective weapon would, perhaps, have been thus eradicated. However, just in the period between defeat and capitulation signing — the future core of the real resistance, under the leadership of the wife of the President of Earth, Helga von Thyssen (in sign of "renunciation of husband's crimes" and by his personal, deathbed instruction — she took her maiden name) managed to qualitatively work with the company's documentation. As a result — a couple hundred rifles turned out to be "never released." Well and in the bardak that reigned — these tricks went unnoticed.

Today — they spoke. As did the assault motorised armours "Vityaz" of Tula BOK production.

No, the storm-troopers weren't old men. They were nearing 50, but all these 20 years — the lads watched themselves, regularly used cykt medicine... And were fully law-abiding citizens... Even with strange hobbies. An Earth officer of regular army would quickly understand that these hobbies are quite easily converted into spec ops skills. There were no Earth officers among the cykts for obvious reasons.

The external perimeter was removed in one salvo. Two dozen Earth insurgents — got special pleasure: ammunition designed for cykts made of significantly less durable Earthlings natural minced meat, which moreover managed to live another ten minutes.

The internal guard didn't differ in number, and consisted predominantly of insurgents (excluding, actually, the prison head — relating likewise to the "Plague-stricken," and his personal bodyguards). Against people, the storm-troopers didn't even bother using rifles — a punch by the force armour's fist was enough for traitors' heads to burst from the blow.

Victoria Stuttgart was discovered in the medical block. The girl was still rather weak after the forced interrogation, but quite alive and not even broken. She was awaiting guests.

The insurgent-doctor was decided to be left alive — after all he saved the leader of the True Resistance. Vika was loaded into an evacuation capsule remaining from the times of former Earth. The smart transport drone began examining its passenger, along the way alertly turning the barrels of two large-calibre machine guns. Damper gravi-engines tore it half a metre from the floor. Now one could go for the second target.

Perrin they found in a far cell. The cykts showed usual humaneness, so the cell was quite comfortable. Up to the fact that even a small kitchenette was organised in it.

François sat on a comfortable couch, and holding a newspaper in one hand — sipped his favourite black coffee. The trained ear of the old warrior couldn't fail to hear the characteristic claps of slugs overcoming the sound barrier. Nevertheless — the admiral was absolutely calm. Hearing OSNAZ already at the doors of his cell — he tore from the newspaper, set the cup on the table and went to the bars.

— Mr. Admiral! Step back, we'll blow the lock now!

— Officers! Atten-shun!

The specs instinctively snapped to attention before the former commander-in-chief. Perrin walked along the bars with hands behind his back, then turned to the soldiers.

— Did you save Vika?

— Yes sir!

— Is she alright?

— Yes sir! Allow us...

— Belay that. I — will remain here. About her — practically no one besides the cykts and secret police of the insurgents, who from misunderstanding consider themselves resisters — knows. The cykts, as their head told me in yesterday's conversation — fled. This Sergei of theirs in general told a lot...

In general — I must stay. Firstly — if I also escape, there'll be much excitement and searches. Secondly — there are quite many sins on me. Thirdly — I'm simply tired of everything. If sometime you can return greatness to Earth — then, if I'm still alive — come back... And yes — my last commission to you...

— Yes, Mr. Admiral!

— See to it that Vika survives and that this Sergei survives. He MUST live to the rebirth of humanity. Or — to its final fall. I — want him punished, but this will be his punishment.

If humanity no longer rises — he'll suffer, because, as I understood, he really, sincerely believed in what he was doing. Both when he grovelled before the cykts — and when he tortured the girl whom, as he confessed once, he loved and idolised. So humanity's final fall — will be torture for him.

Well and if you do pull off Stuttgart's plan... Well that — will also be torture. Because the winner wasn't him, so smart and cunning — who'd devised how to defeat the cykts by ingratiating with them, but ordinary rough warriors. Real resistance.

In general — onward, lads. I hope that plan which Otto didn't even inform me of — you know.

— Will be executed... commander. We — will return for you. The main thing — live, try.

The spec ops retreated just in time. Already descending to the mini-submarines waiting for them near the island — they saw how the combat helicopters that delivered them to the island enter their last and hopeless battle with four flyers of the "plague-stricken" cykt spec ops. The pilots were doomed and knew it. They also firmly knew that getting into the paws of "reborn" little bears was not desirable. There — one of the helicopters having shot its ammo and taken a plasma blaster to the tail stabiliser exploded. Not from the hit — the pilot simply activated the demolition charge. The second managed to hit with "Fury" right into the cockpit of one of the flyers. The explosion cloud tore from the alien machine both glazing shards and pieces of the cykt-pilot. Without control the machine tilted on its side and crashed into Alcatraz's wall. The third flyer gave a burst at the helicopter, no longer ceremoniously and not trying to immobilise it...

At this time — two boats were already mooring to the underwater colossus "Vladimir Putin" — one of four preserved submarines of PKO, refitted during the war into mobile underwater ZKP. The cykts — surprisingly paid little attention to the under-ice world of the North Pole. Luckily for the Resistance, occupying the secret submarine base placed there a couple of centuries ago, built back by the Slavic Union, long before Unification of Nations. Actually, the boat was a legacy of those times, renovated and rebuilt already under Perrin and his patronage...

A week later, already finally on her feet (but still cradling her right hand in a sling) Victoria Stuttgart angrily laughed listening to the speaker's broadcast on WWN channel.

"Thus, our dear friends turned out to be infected. The actions of our children, aimed at earning forgiveness for the sins of our fathers — became, alas, the impulse for the cykts to know the evil hidden in ourselves... That's why yesterday the leader of the Abandoned addressed Humanity with a request for one more, last sacrifice. In return — they offered us technologies of space exploration. So far, the Council of Earth thinks whether we should violate the prohibition imposed by our Departed friends and accept the Abandoned's gift... however — already now there's a need for sacrifices... Luckily — too many sacrifices aren't required from us... At the cost of using the newest technologies — they need only a hundred people a month... Of course — strictly volunteers. People ready to perform a feat for the atonement of the sins of our race!"

— Well, a hundred slugs a month — that's really not many, what do you think, Pete? — Vika addressed her assistant sitting in the adjacent armchair.

— Not many. The trouble is many more will be found. In 20 years — they very nicely brainwashed us. The doubt about the necessity for us of hypertechnologies is worth something...

— Well... Did you contact Rumyantsev?

— Yes. He already managed to break the Council's resistance. Now he and Al Mutanabi — will promote the idea of reviving the post of President. As your parents planned.

— Pity mother didn't live to see. Ready to argue that behind that catastrophe stood Sergei's "Resisters." Damn slug... So Perrin ordered him not to be touched?

— The soldiers say yes. Actually — François's plan is not bad. Very not bad.

— I'll agree. Good. Is the presidential campaign ready? What about my new identity?

— Plastic surgery is already prepared. In a month we land in Murmansk, from there already on a regular flyer to Paris. The face we picked for you — divine. You're a beauty as is (Vika made a gesture "you flatter me, leave it," but judging by her eye expression remained satisfied), and after the operation you simply won't have equal. Over the biography — we're also already working. Right now you're publishing another selection of paintings on the Free Creativity site. Valentin gave his all to create such masterpieces. Then — you've written two hundred articles about friendship and reconciliation just in the last year. You have almost three million followers... Well and next May — you promised to give the first live presentation. Just — all the swellings after plastic will subside.

Documents — of course, ready, dossier — compiled and published. Considering the new appearance and materials prepared by analysts — you can count on the elections on huge sympathies from over three billion citizens. Well and considering the low political activity of the current slugs...

— Let me at least look at who I'll look like. You guessed to synthesise "my" image?

The assistant readily handed Victoria a tablet. Vika long and meticulously examined her future face. A blonde looked at her from the tablet, with huge, enraptured eyes and very long eyelashes. Plump lips were slightly parted, the smooth ideal skin of the face — as if shone from within. The iris — gleamed with an unearthly, cornflower hue. Peter didn't lie. Vika herself fell in love with her new appearance at first sight. Well — there remained to wait until True Resistance agents, Roman Rumyantsev and Said al Mutanabi, who managed to break through into the Council and even become charismatic leaders there, gathering supporters around them and demonstratively holding opposite political views — would push through the idea of President of Earth. Then — power would again return where it belongs: To Real People.

There remained also the problem of the corrupted, also by her efforts, cykts — but it didn't require urgent resolution. All the same — the armed forces she had at the moment were clearly insufficient for destruction of strangers. The slugs definitely wouldn't undertake this. There remained only to wait for a new generation and educate it correctly. Specifically for this matter the Friendship Pansionates project was ready — called to standardise education. The slugs will accept it with joy — it's very tolerant and humane... Well and the curriculum in them... Who'd check? Actually — the "plague-stricken" weren't a serious problem at all. Considering the absence of queens among them — they couldn't multiply. And the cykt individual lifespan was notably shorter than human. Effectively, in one Earth generation — only memories should remain of the cykts. Well and demonstration at the right moment to all slugs of the Fugue of Pain broadcast — was generally part of the Plan. Vika recalled that horror and shuddered. She always left the premises before the start of, actually, the "presentation." However — several times she saw the result... And — heard the inhuman howl of slugs being dissected alive...

By force of will — the future President of Earth pushed away from herself the monstrous memories. From her, still grey eyes — the flashback fog left and they filled with steel. Those who knew both her father and Perrin could have discovered with surprise that this steel was as two drops of water like the steel splashing in critical moments in the Butcher's eyes. Red-headed Otto usually looked at the world with surprised green eyes. However — questions of genealogy, family dramas and student romances no longer mattered.

Victoria Stuttgart, the new President of Earth — was waiting ahead a very lot of work...
