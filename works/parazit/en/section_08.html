Chapter 8

The "whale" entered atmosphere smoothly and as if not noticing monstrous vortexes generated by passage of such massive body. Descended unhurriedly, losing meters five per second, no more.

Humanity, only coming to itself after invasion — bristled. Were urgently brought to combat readiness nuclear forces of Federation. A-135 system around Moscow — homed on "whale."

Nevertheless — we weren't rushing to shoot. Understood that civilization having built such and overcome interstellar abyss — exceeds us by head.

Meanwhile — "whale" hung at five kilometers above earth.

And fell asleep. In sense — stopped giving any signs of life.

We waited day. Then — to whale went research group on helicopters.

Flew up, photographed. Made video recording. Then one of choppers landed on "whale" from above.

And then "whale" came alive.

Moscow was shaken by low-frequency hum. How A-135 operators didn't turn keys in fright — I don't know.

And then, in air, over city, appeared angel.

Real one — shining with white and golden light. Spreading wings of incredible beauty snow-white. At same time — all turned on radios and televisions on earth — showed him too.

And angel spoke in all world languages simultaneously:

— Life to you, children! You — are worthy.

— We — are Masters.

— Yours and Universe's.

— We — are judges.

— We — are fate.

— You passed test.

— You earned reward.

— You are worthy.

Angels silent, giving humanity time to grasp what was happening. While most people came to themselves — something strange happened to President Simonov.

Alexei Anatolyevich, who from start of broadcast grew gloomier and gloomier — at end jumped up, and waving hand to personal guard rushed to exit from crisis center.

I considered no more than ten seconds, and then went after. Rest, looks like, didn't even notice First President of Federation's disappearance.

Alexei rushed as if turpentined, clearly trying to make somewhere in time. Rising to surface — looked around and dashed to nearest police car. By time I caught up with him — he already twirled in hands powerful police megaphone, given to him by dazed patrol officer. Guard knew me perfectly, so let through without conversation.

— Sergei Anatolyevich — what's happening?

— Andrey Mikhailovich? Glad you're here. I don't believe them. Right before broadcast — choppers transmitted that this thing's hull is biological, yes?

— Yes. There are my guys right now. Dense skin cover. There are noticeable pores. Across skin periodically run convulsive contractions.

— Well. You yourself understand — attack of biological parasite and appearance of biological civilization — these are links of one chain!

— And don't you think, Mr. President, that this could be banal galactic sanepidnadzor? Flew in, you see, my colleagues?

— I think. And also I think it would be good to check this. Oh, and here's helicopter.

Chopper landed not far from bunker entrance. Simonov gave couple orders to pilots, after which returned to cabin and set about tuning megaphone.

Meanwhile, chopper went in arc under "whale." Approximately to that place where angel was broadcast. Between heaven and earth.

Flying up roughly to head of holographic "angel" — chopper went into hover. Alexei strapped himself to frame with safety line and rolled aside side door. After which turned megaphone to full and addressed plate:

— You say we passed test. And brought it on us — also you?!

For minute — nothing happened. I mentally smirked — idea of yelling at "whale" hanging two kilometers from us frankly didn't look sensible.

Nevertheless minute later — answer sounded.

— Yes, Test is our brainchild.

— Do you even imagine how many people died? How many lives you took?!!!

— Yes. It doesn't matter.

— Yes are you nuts there?! — even our born politician and orator fell into groggy state.

— Your species — only sand grain in sea of stars. You are insignificant. Nevertheless — you are intelligent. And had to pass exam on right to exist. Such are our rules. You — passed exam. Have right to live. Have right to stars. Maybe sometime, will inherit universe. In these scales, life of billions doesn't matter.

— You killed millions of children. You destroyed our nature! Radioactive winds of America's north, burned forests of India...

— Your larvae represent no value. You'll produce more. Your nature you destroyed yourselves. Our gifts — will compensate this.

— Tell me — President squinted — and what if we now strike on you nuclear blow with everything we have?

— If it returns you peace of mind — please. This won't cause us harm. Though — in this case we may reconsider our assessment of your adequacy. And therefore exam results in general.

— Yes who the hell are you!

— We — are ancient. We are Masters of this galaxy. We are older than your very time itself. We are immortal. We watch order. We test races of intelligent on right to life and right to exit into space. Such is our calling. We — are architects of this world.

— Did someone appoint you?

— We appointed ourselves. We — are strongest. We — are seniors. We — remember what you never knew. We — are highest.

— We — are Masters.

— Accept our gifts.

President — just waved hand.

— We give you Servants. These creatures will obey everyone in whom is our blessing.

— Are they about parasite? — surprised Alexei.

— Looks like... Blessed us... From soul...

— We give you health and longevity. Our blessing, henceforth, won't turn you into Warriors of Test. It will protect you and serve you. Henceforth you will live long, you won't get sick. Your species will acquire abilities of Warriors — you'll be able to heal even lost parts of your fragile bodies.

— Maybe still bash with nuclear club, eh? — mournfully inquired president. Both bodyguards sadly nodded, and "nuclear officer" turned "briefcase" to chief. I significantly cleared throat. President twitched cheek, waved to officer and again stared at "Whale." Pilots at this time brought "chopper" to "snout."

— You lost much passing Test. We give you technology that will give you more energy than you need. You'll be able to quickly restore your losses.

— Also — we give you stars. You'll receive technology of interstellar movement.

— You passed Test and you turned out worthy. Let your leader rise to us and take everything that's due you.

We — exchanged glances. And next second at "whale" opened "mouth." Simonov shrugged shoulders, after which jabbed tangent on helmet and briefly ordered: "Forward." Presidential "orca" — smoothly slipped into "maw" of alien ship.
