Chapter 9

...Underfoot smoothly stretched pile. Judging by everything ship was structured in such way to absorb all possible waste. To judge about structure of such huge organism — I didn't undertake. Clear was only that this isn't fully biological device. Through flesh — sprouted steel beams, wires. "Whale" was cyborg.

President walked absolutely confidently. Behind nervously walked guard. "Nuclear officer" we left in chopper, along with pilots. To guard, and not needed he was inside enemy starship. I, as biologist — tagged along after big boss.

Ahead — trotted Servant. Yes, that very spider-pony. Looks like McManus's assumptions about their universal nature were correct. We passed through several rooms, turned, turned again. For some reason intra-ship transport wasn't provided. Moreover — sections themselves looked abandoned.

Another couple doors later — we found ourselves in semicircular chamber with ugly growth on ceiling. Exactly there waited us angel. Though — Simonov already had bit between teeth, and as knew everyone who was acquainted with him, in this state his armor-piercing was off scale.

— So. We came. Be so kind, remove all your mysticism. I want to see your real appearance. I want to look in eyes of those guilty of deaths of billions of people. Well or whatever you have instead of eyes?

— Don't you feel fear and awe?

— I feel serious irritation and barely restrained hatred for killers. Show yourself!

— Well alright...

Angel disappeared. Cocoon on ceiling cracked along seam, after which with squelching sound parted and from there fell whitish worm length of two human heights.

One end of worm, thinner, remained in cocoon. He clung by it apparently. Or just grew in. Thicker end ended with small head, darker against background of disgusting whitish, ringed carcass. Right under head from torso grew some kind of tentacles... Or pseudopodia. Hell knew. I decided not to hang earthly biological stamps on this nastiness.

Creature bent in letter U raising free end of torso to level of Alexei's head. President turned green, but so far restrained. Creature was devoid of face. Nevertheless on head one could clearly see huge eyes. There was no mouth, apparently fed from tail.

— As I see — you refused our blessing. Did all residents of planet already do this?

— Not yet. But we have all possibilities.

— There's no need for this. Unfortunately, as I see, you can't again receive blessing. I feel sorry for you. However those who still preserved it — will receive our gift of life and health. Also — only they will be able to communicate with Servants.

— Are those creatures that hatch from eggs of Soldiers? — clarified just in case I.

— Yes — creature looked at me — you scientist? That's good. You also refused blessing? Strange. Scientist should have understood prospects. You — chose strange path of solution.

— What do you mean?

— Test was passed by thousands of others. Very many perished. Then, their planet by Servants was turned into blooming garden. In galaxy thousands of such planets. They wait, when will come those who passed tests — will come to take them for themselves. Some — passed tests.

Such weren't many. But as rule decision was different than yours. Someone assimilated Soldiers. Someone using features of society structure — quenched epidemic in embryo. Someone won in war. But only you — found our secret switch. Could use unprovided solution. We even doubted — to count this test for you. You — as if knew that in Soldiers is built switch. Are you familiar with such method?

— We assumed no one would make weapons without "stop" button.

— Good.

— Planet leader. We award you these recordings.

Servant stepped forward. In hands he had... parallelepiped? Well I don't know how to describe this. Suitcase without handles, without zipper and without hint that it opens? Material was light and at first glance reminded ivory. Dimensions were quite decent. Though, looks like, he floated through air himself. As soon as Alexei touched top face — it melted. Inside — was dense stack of some sheets. I approached and slightly pulled one out. Judging by everything this was some composite, like graphene with titanium threads or something similar. On its surface — were engraved formulas and some drawings. Main thing I noted — language, where there was one — was earthly. Russian. Besides — all formulas were clearly in our standard. I pushed sheet back and ran hand along side face. One of them. From face protruded some semblance of handle. Pulled "suitcase" easily followed me.

— Our meeting is concluded. Maybe, sometime we'll see you again between stars.

— Oh! Don't doubt! And, I think, this will be quite soon — grinned President.

— This is your right. You passed test. You proved your right to life and independence. What now to spend this life on — decide you and only you. Including you can try to find us.

— A moment — can I ask one more question? — I tried to catch moment.

— Ask, scientist.

— You say "we" — there are many of you? What are you?

— We — are race. We are ancient. Once, possibly, we resembled you. Or maybe Servants. Or maybe Lkhashi. Who knows. It doesn't matter. We grew. We subjugated Galaxy to ourselves. Laws of physics and chemistry serve us.

In youth — we are mobile. We research worlds belonging to us. Learn. With age — we become bored on planet. We join family.

— Each ship — is family, am I right? — it dawned on me.

— You're right. One ship — is one family. For millennia we research universe. We search for such as you. We conduct tests. We save pre-intelligent species from death — so test could then be passed by them too. Our goal — world in which won't be weakness and indecision. World — inhabited only by worthy races. World of brave and strong. Conversation — is over.

...As soon as our helicopter departed from "Whale," it began movement. Smoothly, but increasingly gaining speed — it went toward ocean.

Later, from satellites, we saw as it descended into Atlantic. As water in terrible whirlpool rushed into its womb.

Several hours later — "whale" likewise smoothly rose into air. Took echelon about 25 kilometers, after which began gaining speed, moving around planet. Then — began sowing behind itself some chemical cloud.

Another twelve hours later — left atmosphere boundaries and went into space. Moving thousand kilometers from Earth — simply disappeared.

At this time — I studied analyses and tomogram already of tenth person subjected to chemical treatment.

Parasite changed. Now — its main mass consisted exactly of Hippocratic glands. Nervous tissue remained little. Apparently exactly as much as needed parasite for active medical monitoring.

Cut on test subject healed in time from three seconds (cut depth up to 2 millimeters, damage to upper epidermis layers) to 20 minutes (cut depth — 4 centimeters, soft tissues affected. Deeper we didn't cut. Already everything was clear). Corpse of previously sentenced to death for marauding subject — gave no signs of life for already three hours. Biomonitoring stated that protein denaturation processes, cell death and autolysis and other characteristic for death changes — go entirely routinely.

United group physicists — pored over graphite tablets (with material I did guess). Russian colleagues hastily made translations. Materials were very much.

Three days later — was announced lifting of quarantine and chemical alarm. People came out, inhaled new air saturated with another chemical command. And became immortal.

African research expedition — confirmed fact of death of remnants of observed praetorians (to depths of Africa we hadn't yet managed to reach with sprayers, so scientists just sprayed themselves. This sufficed for praetors to not come closer than kilometer).

Servants on the contrary — established contact. To researchers' surprise turned out Servants can even speak in human languages. In researched area main language turned out to be Afrikaans. Looks like this had relation to assimilation of carrier's brain.

Now — Servants became our faithful helpers. Moreover turned out that unlike praetorians, servants to radiation practically insensitive. So now, around world, these creatures occupied themselves restoring order after invasion.

Earth — entered golden era.

Only Masters didn't understand one very simple truth — we never and nothing forgot.

And now, erecting shining cities, birthing many, from birth practically immortal children, building whole armada of first wave research and colonization starships — we breathed not at all communist ideas of "peace throughout world."

No. We were driven by hatred and revenge.

Our colonization starships — were hung with weapons more than battleships of World War II times.

Our shining cities — stood on ultra-durable bunkers. And monuments in squares hid linear particle accelerators, which became available to us with new energy technologies.

Antigravity, discovered by us independently as side effect from hyperdrive — was used not only in light, seemingly-flying machines of future, absorbing design of 50s and human faith in nearness of space, but also in chassis of superheavy tanks.

Fissile materials freed with arrival of energy abundance — were rolled into warheads of titanic power. 50 megatons? Don't make us laugh. Main caliber missiles of deep search cruisers (ships of second wave, which now were hastily assembled in deep underground bunkers) — had power of 500 megatons. And this, by the way, wasn't limit.

Masters could notice strangeness but couldn't understand it. We — weren't "standard" civilization. Humans were unique. Masters not in vain hesitated with credit. Right decision for them would have been to destroy us. To us remained quite little.

They didn't do this.

Now — we prepared to pay our debts.

Earth — always pays its bills.

They never understood that they with their own hands created civilization of perfect killers. Devoid of template, devoid of frames. Fanatical, completely consumed by idea of revenge.

Yes, gap between us was huge. Yes, this was ancient civilization and we were young descendants of monkeys — just-barely exiting into space.

But we were united and they weren't.

Even ants are capable of bringing down and devouring elephant. Main thing is to have mandibles and numerical superiority.

Nuclear mandibles we'd already forged. Number — likewise grew in geometric progression.

Within framework of state program — Ministry of Medicine developed powerful fertility increase preparations. Now, woman in one go gave birth to four to six children. Marriage age was sharply lowered. Now women married at 12. Feminism, homosexuality — were treated in psychiatric clinics. Boys from very childhood, from kindergartens, from schools — were raised as soldiers. They had no toy cars — had tanks. They had no computer games — had almost real assault rifles, shooting first soft rubber balls, and by school already steel balls leaving quite serious scratches. School program underwent significant changes. Increased hours of instruction, differential equations went already from third grade and at biology lessons children were given basics of biological weapons action.

Federation — quickly and confidently transformed into Military Empire. President Simonov, long retired, convinced democrat before invasion — effectively himself laid beginning of this transformation.

When we met ashloks — we'd been colonizing space for 20 years already. At that moment we didn't yet know whether enemies or friends these were. But in people's heads firmly sat clinical xenophobia.

So first meeting with us became unforgettable event for ashloks.
