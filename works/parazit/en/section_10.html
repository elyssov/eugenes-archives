Chapter 10

Destruction of Ashlok ship — was caused by civilizational error. Effectively, features of their technology of planet colonization — became death sentence for 100,000 colonists flying on "Nag-Jfar."

In 2056, deep search cruiser "Socrates" searching for native world of Masters — jumped out of hyper near another "pearl." Or planet of class "Eden" as they called them in Admiralty. Another legacy from Masters. Another perished civilization. Killed civilization.

According to protocol 3-12, "Socrates" immediately ejected courier shuttle and itself transitioned to patrol on orbit. Three days later — arrived duty colonization ship: "Magellan."

Of course immediately upon arrival began standard procedures: Landing of trial party, study by duty ISCD group of ruins of former civilization (Yes, since those times when I served humanity — much changed. So my service now was called Imperial Service of Quarantine Defense). All ISCD work time — all 10,000 of first colonist party were on orbit. Here must say at once that any earth colonization ship represented flying fortress. Nevertheless — until colonists were on planet, all VKS fighters were in extremely paranoid state. Therefore, when on scanners was detected hyper-transition signature not fitting parameters of earth drives — on "Socrates" played combat alarm. And "Magellan," though crept under "Socrates" hull protection, but opened rocket silos and connected with "Socrates" BIUS to receive directions whom, when and how to hit.

Hyper funnel unfolded and spat out into near-planet space foreign ship. Even apparatus design betrayed extraterrestrial origin. Bundle of spherical modules, connected by openwork trusses and tubular passages — was attached by long power structure to rectangular, with beveled corners. From rectangle to sides went "wings" ending, judging by everything, in chemical engines. Ship jumped out of hyperspace, kicked in full power its engines, giving multi-meter flame torches, and dashed to planet surface.

BCh-4 and BCh-6 — reported on target capture. BCh-3 and BCh-5 — reported on their readiness to shoot.

At this time, stranger opened four rows of hatches — one on each long face of rectangular section.

Atmosphere in citadel of Earth Empire cruiser — sparked.

Minute later — potentially enemy ship launched hundred cylindrical objects, which weaving smoky trails from engines — rushed to planet... And to earth ships.

Further was predictable.

Seconds later — PRO systems launched dozens of missiles at enemy small targets. Well and toward enemy ship rushed bursts from automatic, 305 millimeter guns. To full power kicked in gravity engines, compensating recoil. In case main caliber's eight guns didn't suffice — both earth ships prepared launching heavy missiles with half-gigaton warheads.

Though, stranger sufficed first three hits. Three of twenty-four fired. Enemy ship, despite that in linear dimensions it significantly exceeded even half-kilometer cruiser, approaching three-kilometer "Magellan" — was torn to shreds. Looks like, it carried no armor.

87 launched objects — were likewise destroyed. Nevertheless — 13 entered atmosphere. On this matter — ISCD group was warned, and from board of "Magellan" was immediately sent landing boat, crammed with marines....

...200 captured by our fighters colonists — were delivered to Earth already in two days.

Me, by old memory, called to MO. I by this moment had long not appeared in public. Couple years before this, Simonov died of old age. I too grew decrepit — dictated articles to granddaughter who became effectively my assistant and secretary.

Actually — exactly in MO I learned above-described history. And at same time saw first neutral aliens. With them already worked specialists of military intelligence. Three — laid out in department of biological research.

Three legs, with double joints. Rudimentary wings. By earth measures — we again dealt with insects. I winced and didn't look in more detail.

To Admiral Greyhawk I right away said that to help substantially I couldn't with anything. In response John smirked with one half of mouth and explained that for services to civilization — I have right to be in center of events. Especially since besides textbooks — I write a bit memoirs, as became known to him.

As result — Alice and I were given apartments in residential building of Admiralty and given "everywhere passes" with right of access to Control Room. So from this moment — I became semi-official chronicler of operation. First Conquests of Humanity.

So, on finding common tongue with aliens — we needed ten days and two corpses.

Another four — went on our captives forgetting phrases "I'm intelligent being," "demand humane treatment," "This is madness" and other stupidities. Two of them had to be burned in sealed containers. Too unappetizing spectacle came out.

Our vis-à-vis went into contact with huge eagerness and desire. At any rate exactly until they understood we were interested in coordinates of their native planet. After this — they en masse fell ill with terminal stage of heroism.

By this moment we already knew error had happened, that we shot down colonization ship, that they simply didn't notice us, that on ship itself were about 100,000 individuals in cryocapsules...

And all this — was absolutely insignificant. Earth Empire urgently needed victory.

As result — another two dozen lives later, using map of nerve nodes compiled by medics-xenobiologists — we got coordinates too.

May 6, 2056 by Earth Empire reckoning — skies of Ash collapsed in fire. First on ground fell debris of orbital shipyards and stations. Following — bombs burning out bridgeheads.

Then, EI cruisers — occupied strike positions over major cities of Ashloks' mother world.

While on prepared bridgeheads — landed marines with heavy equipment, Admiral Greyhawk, from board of flagship "Wrath of Humanity" (first in series of superheavy battleships — ships of new, third generation, monster almost six kilometers in length and kilometer in width, with armor three meters thick and eating energy more than whole Earth spent in day before Invasion) addressed united government of ashloks.

Democratic and peace-loving alien roaches, demoralized by might of invasion — surrendered unconditionally, recognizing full power of Earth Empire over themselves. Must say, we turned out generous hosts. In particular — we didn't set ourselves goal to destroy them, rob. We were interested only in their production capacities and their knowledge. The latter, though, turned out to be nothing.

Although... Although exactly from them we managed to learn coordinates of our second victim: native planet of arcadians — Selestia. Cat-like, big-eared — they strongly resembled "Na-vi" from early-century "Avatar," except they were significantly smaller and far more peaceful. I'd say — completely harmless. Surprisingly, these friends survived Test by simply sitting it out in underground bunkers. Naive. They thought they could sit it out there from us too...

Earth Empire — was gathering strength.

We didn't yet know that someone disliked this very much.

Though, as known, rhinoceros is short-sighted, but with its mass and hide — these aren't its problems.

At this moment, thickness of hide, length of horn and mass — were being built up at striking pace by eight planets. On approach were two dozen earth colonies entering industrial regime. Through raising birth rate and militarization of society — at present moment, in sum with colonies we'd already reached 4 billion human population. Increasing it each year by exponent.

Worms — committed grandiose error leaving us alive. Apparently, whole universe would have to pay for this error.

Well and I — each year more and more grew disappointed in path chosen by humanity. We, looks like, were turning into futuristic semblance of Roman Empire — in particular, in more-or-less wealthy families came into fashion arcadian slaves. No, this wasn't called slavery directly. However — was it by fact, strongly resembling popular in Russia in early century "Thai servants." Considering kindness, obedience and disciplinedness of arcadians — to real slavery, looks like, remained counted steps.
