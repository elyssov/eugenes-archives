Chapter 12

I write these lines... I myself don't know why. This is testimony which no one will ever read. This is tribute to memory of a man who will never be remembered.

This is monument to those who still live and, most likely, will live very, very long...

Pointlessly, but...

My name is Alice. Alice Pirogova.

I — am the last mortal human on Earth.

This — is my story. This — is story of all earthlings.

Appearance of armada mixed our cards, but didn't knock us out of saddle. I at that moment was with my grandfather — as aide and secretary.

In his notes he noticeably understated his merits and his role at that moment. Suffice to say that he was personal advisor of young Emperor.

Grandfather died in my arms half an hour before news came from Berlin. Negotiations didn't end successfully. Alien Alliance considered worms as Gods. Alliance considered Test was worth it. Alliance didn't give damn about victims, about death of most of biosphere of their planets.

They were satisfied with price given them as compensation.

Having discovered such egregious meanness of Alliance representatives — we wrapped up negotiations. In any case, time for pulling up strike fleet we won.

So, when in rear of "pacification" fleet began one after another opening hyper-transition portals and from there began emerging combat ships of second and third generations — aliens had nothing left but to sell their lives more dearly.

Must admit. They cost us quite dear.

Alien ships exceeded us numerically. They exceeded us also technically. Where we used mainly kinetic weapons — they preferred beam and energy.

Only they didn't take into account courage of our crews and that our VKS were built more by patterns of armor fleet of early 20th century, than in paradigm of aviation.

Medium paa-zu cruiser — represented sphere of "foil" wrapped in powerful force fields absorbing energetics of plasma impulse or laser beam and stuffed with generators and heavy laser cannons.

Medium cruiser of second generation of Earth Fleet — represented elongated armored three-meter-thick plates of refractory steel tube, in principle deprived of any force fields, with electronics simple by galactic measures, without artificial intelligence, but to make up for it studded with batteries of automatic guns, 200 years ago considered main caliber for battleships. Guns shooting most ordinary armor-piercing shells stuffed with kilograms of explosive.

Our ships didn't possess range or maneuverability of enemy. But they were monstrously tenacious, almost invulnerable to their weapons and we managed to catch them in trap, driving into space between planet surface and our strike fleet.

Lasers in vain bit into armor of cruisers and battleships — even galactics didn't have enough power to saw through thick armor, and clouds of evaporated metal after first hit created reliable, absorbing up to 90% of radiation curtain. Along the way, by the way, carrying off considerable share of heat brought by beam — extremely important moment in vacuum.

On other hand — powerful guns of cruisers and monstrous cannons of battleships, equipped with nuclear charges — tore apart enemy ships even at slight miss.

Of course Gaapt couldn't not understand catastrophic nature of fight on our conditions — and tried to outplay us, bringing ships into operational space and breaking away by better engines and maneuver.

Unfortunately for him — Earth General Staff was exactly waiting for this maneuver from Gaapt. Breaking out of near-Earth space fleet — met by salvo of gigaton missiles from monitors, prudently occupying positions in nearest transition points yet during battle and from then, with muffled engines and radars, drifting in emptiness. Hard gamma radiation, in vacuum conditions absorbed by nothing — tore force fields from ships and burned them from inside, leaving only beautiful but empty shell...

Though — not everyone was so lucky. Those who turned out close enough — were crushed by gamma radiation itself.

Gaapt himself — seeing what was happening, blew up his ship. His example was followed by some number of survivors. Apparently — he gave order.

However — if you're immortal, and at that not obsessed with thirst for revenge, like we, earthlings — dying becomes hard. Therefore — majority of survivors (one and half dozen of two hundred originally arrived) ships of enemy — surrendered. Example of ashloks and arcadians — taught them nothing.

Though, as said above — price turned out indeed huge. We lost up to 90% of ships of first fleet. Remained still Second and Third — but about invasion to worms had to temporarily forget.

Next three decades — were spent on conquest of "Confederation of Five." In all — 23 planets. Looks like, of all civilizations that passed Test — only we strove to embrace as many worlds as possible with our presence. In galaxy by this moment were already 15 purely human colonies and number continued to grow. At same time — most known to us races limited to two-three colonies and led very pastoral way of life. Excellent example in this sense were arcadians.

Having high intellect, being excellent scientists — they continued to live in mud huts with minimal set of conveniences. And this when their science — in many aspects exceeded ours.

For these thirty years — much changed. Arcadians finally became slaves de jure. Earth, having received Confederation technologies — advanced in science forward by hundreds of years. Force fields, teleportation, beam weapons... Only thing in pen was medicine. Symbiont — diligently watched over health of its carriers. People didn't get sick, didn't suffer, restored crippled organs like lizards. In general — there was no need for medicine. Moreover — given loss of natural reservoirs, many diseases over these years simply died out.

Army was transformed into Legion.

And, contrary to grandfather's forecasts — Earth still lived by revenge.

From clash with united Alliance fleet — we drew right conclusions. And now whole Confederation churned out weapons for us.

I — already twenty years served in fleet. There too found my chosen one. Yan Kiskalis over this time became first-rank captain, received under his command battleship of fourth generation... By irony of fate it was named "Mark McManus" — in honor of grandfather's comrade. In general, exploit of doctors and other FCD employees — humanity remembered. In fleet were immortalized all any notable figures of that time. Came even to corvette "Granin," named in honor of their public relations officer, it seems.

Until wedding — remained still half a year. By Yan's data, until start of invasion implementation — year.

I — went to Earth to prepare everything for celebrations. Yan — was going with his ship on maneuvers, after which together with rest of expeditionary fleet — was to return to Earth. Just for our wedding.

In pleasant chores — time flew imperceptibly. Mother had long given grandfather's house in my full disposal, father — promised to arrive for celebration: being fleet admiral — he was fully occupied at shipyards. Receiving "Pirogov" — this honor was entrusted to him as son. After all first battleship of fifth generation — absorbing technological achievements of all races of Empire.

I just chased my three arcadians around house when Azara — young and terribly cute girl, with tender, lilac fur (I perfectly understood that arcadians were intelligent beings not animals, but couldn't not pet them, perceiving subconsciously as huge cats. Though they themselves liked it up to borrowed from earth cats purring) — dashed to me excitedly waving tail:

— Mamma, oh, we found such, found such!!!!! — arcadian couldn't even stand still from excitement, jumped like crazy squirrel.

— We bottle, and it pop! And wall — whoosh!!!! And there! Oh! Let's-let's-let's-let's go! — slave grabbed me by hand and insistently pulled down. Into wine cellar.

...No, probably it still wasn't secret room. More precisely — not secret room in familiar sense. Grandfather just didn't want to be disturbed.

Room, or rather full lab, was equipped with latest technology of its time. I was never specialist in medicine, so could identify not all devices. But judging by everything there was both tomograph and X-ray and some scanners. Was separate sectional too. Hell — he even had small morgue! Thank god empty.

Separately stood some strange aggregate covered with cover.

We with girls pulled cover and discovered this was some kind of couch, with head holder and large, apparently emitting device over this holder.

Ursa, oldest of my slaves, who came to earth already in comparatively mature age and had in her world engineering education — immediately stuck curious nose into device. I at first wanted to give her on ears, but then decided extra information wouldn't hurt me. Though — to attract her attention had still to pull her by tail.

— Oh!! Mamma?!

— Don't get too carried away — my grandfather was great scientist. Hell knew what he could have left here. Suddenly this rips heads off arcadians?

— Oh-oh! — Azara and Huura pulled in ears and tucked tails. Ursa — just smirked.

— Don't be afraid, I'll figure it out and tell you everything. Can't allow my mistress to be so beautiful but so uneducated? — what to answer to insolent one I didn't find, so snorting climbed into desk. And — found there diary.

First entries I didn't find interesting. Pile of formulas, calculations, drafts of symbiont's DNA chains, judging by four threads...

But last was fully text... And I became interested:

"Looks like, I was right. With parasite everything isn't simple. Thus, glial cells form outgrowths looking like antennas. Yes — we don't know natural constructions capable of receiving radio waves. But how much did we know in general before invasion? I researched many people. Friends treat my eccentricities skeptically, but allow old man to amuse himself with research. All of them — didn't refuse to undergo examination. As I assumed — on CT clearly visible parasite develops. Cells again embed into brain.

Yes, so far we don't feel this, and growing it extremely slowly. But what does time mean for immortal, at any rate biologically, race?

Again — how easily aliens accept our command without single attempt to revolt.... On my equipment I researched several dozen bodies of ashloks and arcadians. In all cases their symbiont — practically fully grew into one brain department.

Couple months ago — I conducted experiment: assembled radioectomizing apparatus and ran through it one ashlok and one arcadian. In both cases effect was incredible. Ashlok — rushed at me as soon as came to himself after operation. I barely managed to shoot him. Arcadian — went wild, got brazen. Unlike ashlok throwing himself at me he didn't — apparently understood I'd manage to shoot first. But behaved defiantly, demanded stop treating him as lower being...

All results I passed to Chancellery. Next day corpse of ashlok and arcadian, stormily protesting against this, were taken.

I — begin to understand plan. Week ago we, having discussed everything with Masha — conducted ectomy on her too. We now have difference of 40 years. Amazing. I'm already deep old man, and she looks like same young girl. Now true — will grow up.

Need to convince everyone. Need to convey to everyone. But — how to do this? Best bait in world — is exactly immortality. How to make people refuse it?

Though — maybe I'm worrying for nothing? Old man's paranoia — not most rare phenomenon in world..."

Diary on this broke off. I set it aside and pondered.

— Hmm... In general this is device for conducting some radiation therapy. Very local, must say — Ursa finished examination of device — And in your grandfather's diary is nothing?

— There is, but something incomprehensible. Here, familiarize yourself if you want. — I held out to Ursa the book and went to meet workers, who were to construct stage and temporary altar.

...Fleet ships arrived next day. Guests at grandfather's villa — began arriving from very morning. First, of course, closest relatives and friends, who volunteered to help. Somewhere disappeared my arcadian slaves, but, in haste I didn't immediately notice this...

...And then, on Earth's orbit — again appeared "whale." Exactly such-same appeared over all earth colonies.

Most likely we were too taken aback by such impudence, therefore didn't open fire right away. And then became late.

This time — voices, dozens of voices, sounded at each of us under cranial lid:

— We greet you, Children.

— You — grew up.

— You, finally, successful result.

— Thousands of worlds — and each time, insufficient malice, fury, ambition.

— Cosmos — too peaceful place. Here no one is ready to fight. No one possesses sufficient aggressiveness.

— Sufficient changeability of consciousness.

— Sufficient persistence.

— You — are unique.

— You are needed by us.

— We wage war. We wage many wars. Much blood. We need soldiers.

— We give to your full power lesser nations. You conquered them anyway. Symbiont made them submissive to you. Now — they will become simply extension of your hands.

— As are you — of ours.

— You won't be slaves, what you.

— We'll just become your gods. As was conceived. You no longer need freedom of choice. You don't need your former life. Obey and be happy!

And world exploded! And I felt happiness and peace I'd never known before this time.

Over our world hovered God! He was Love and all of us were Children his!

He — condescended to us! He blessed us!

Even before his words I knew my duty — to bring on this earth as many children as I can, as health will allow me! Health given by Them in their infinite Love to us!

I looked around and saw Johan Steiner, old friend of Yan. I saw he was fit for performance of duty. He too saw me... And then light flashed even brighter, I felt strong pain in back of head and lost consciousness....

...Return to world of living was agonizing. Head ached at once both in back and at base of skull. In eyes slightly doubled.

I — was strapped to that very stand, face down. Through hole — I could see bare feet. Judging by bluish hue of fur this was Ursa.

— Did you come to, Alice? Don't deceive, I feel.

— Yes... What's happening???

— You'll be heroine of our Motherland. You, and, of course, your grandfather. But before — let's check something. Tell me how do you relate to Masters?

— To whom? You about worms???! Oh god.... — my memory filled with fragments of sensations from contact with Gods... Or more precisely with Worms. I winced.

— What was that?! What... vileness!

— Glory to goddess — you're alright, young mistress. Though... Let's at once agree, that with all these "mistresses" and "slaveries" we're done. I now perceive you rather as daughter. Now.

— Hey! What's happening to you, Ursa!!!

— Freedom. With me and with other girls. And this is only beginning. Let me unstrap you and help move to couch. And we'll talk calmly. You promise there won't be excesses?

— Fine... — I was unstrapped. Soft paws-hands of arcadians (yes, there were about ten of them here — three mine and six-eight guests'. Head spun, so it was hard to count exactly) — moved me to couch. Then, arcadians dispersed around grandfather's lab, and opposite me remained sitting in chair Ursa. Something in her changed. Elusively... But I could no longer perceive her as big, two-legged cat. Something told me she wouldn't purr anymore either.

Ursa softly smiled at me.

— I'm glad that you, seems, understood everything. Yes. I read your grandfather's notes and decided to put experiment on myself. Persuaded girls. We did it. First with me, then with them. Then — with rest we could reach. We — are free. We can decide ourselves what we do. No more barriers... It's hard to explain, but our slavery, our passivity — were from outside. They were provided by symbiont, for some reason decided to subjugate us to you.

— I... seems know why... We turned out to be peak of Masters' selection.

— Good. We managed shortly before arrival of Masters' ship. You know, Alice, over these years — we all sincerely came to love you. Yes, you were our mistress and periodically confused us with your dolls, or with house animals... But you were sincerely warm with us. You loved us, and we — couldn't not answer you in kind. So, seeing that you no longer belong to yourself — we kidnapped you and conducted on you same operation.

— Ursa! Girls! Forgive me for everything! What dears you are! Turns out not everything is lost?! Together with you we can save all other people?! Will catch one by one... For wedding gathered cream of fleet, if we manage to return to ranks these captains...

Ursa grew gloomy, looked at me somehow too seriously, after which sighed and spoke. And each her word — cut like razor blade.

— No, Alice. There won't be any "we." We love you, but we don't love people. Your species is malicious, cruel, selfish, vengeful.

You — were befallen by expected punishment. And we wouldn't want, that people regained independence again — you're simply dangerous for galaxy.

We — are returning home. This device — we'll dismantle and take with us. Now reigns terrible confusion, and arcadian slaves — are encountered on earth everywhere. I think we'll be able to steal small cargo ship. You — can go with us. Then we'll guarantee you happy life on our planet. We'll pick up and heal for you male of your race, you'll be able to happily live out your age among friends.

Either — you can stay here. Humanity didn't perish and apparently isn't going to perish. It just becomes different. Loses its freedom. Including freedom of will. If you want — you can try to heal people. Find way to fight parasite. Everything — is in your hands. You — preserved your freedom of will. But — in this case, our paths diverge.

Azara came to me, got on knees before couch, butted me in shoulder with her cat head and purred, after which sadly looked in eyes:

— Alis, well come with us... you understand, here everything ended. And with us — it's good! I'll introduce you to my mom... You know, I haven't seen her for twenty years...

— Really, Alice, come! We'll be friends! We'll care for you, like you once did for us! — Huura joined Azara, taking me by hand and stroking shoulder.

— She won't go — through tears running from my eyes, I discerned Ursa — she too can't drop everything. And we — already need to run. Remember, girl — if you ever need home, our planets will always be glad to receive you. And now goodbye. If will of Goddess will be on it — we'll meet again...

Slaves and slave-girls... First free arcadians — slipped shadows out door, taking with them unique device that could have been salvation of humanity. Door slammed. I — remained alone.

...Now thirty years have passed. Behind youth. Behind dreams. Behind life.

Me — they allow to exist. People of new formation have — brain structured in such way that they, looks like, don't notice me.

But — I finally saw Worms. Surprisingly, they aren't worms. Looks like, image shown to Simonov was specially selected to evoke maximum negative reaction from possible.

Masters — are humanoids. Very similar to us, except more refined and noticeably taller. In height almost three meters. Beautiful but inhuman faces, protruding shoulder levers. Strange and alien creatures.

People — no longer independent race. People — religious cosmos fanatics. Masters' temples on every corner. Frenzy.

Children are raised at monasteries. Raised as warriors and scientists. Women — these are no longer women. Their legs almost atrophy by age 12. Just by puberty. They turned our women into queens. Queens — birthing soldiers and other queens. I took samples, as far as I can judge — problem isn't in DNA. They're changed from inside by parasite.

Almost all men over 15 — are constantly at war. Before this — live in monasteries. Those who survive in crucible for 10 years — become mentors for young for another 10 years. About 10 percent — are sent to labs, to invent new weapons, new technologies. Strange, but Worms don't give their developments to people, demanding from them to develop science themselves. Although they set direction of developments. Productive labor isn't engaged in by single earthling.

Economy? What's this funny word?

All earthlings' needs — are provided by servants. Servants — are former Alliance of Five. They build ships, cook and clean in monasteries, grow food, care for queens.... And at that — idolize earthlings. Mechanism is exactly same. For right to clean shit after queen — paa-zu arrange fights with often fatal outcomes. And then obtained product — use for ritual purposes, applying on themselves bizarre patterns.

I — increasingly rarely leave grandfather's lab. I spend all my time on developing parasitoectomy methodology anew. I independently studied everything possible on radiotherapy, neurosurgery, all materials on parasites. Unfortunately — very much data was lost: all information from our networks, Masters deleted first thing. I use what was preserved in grandfather's computer memory, in his notes. Damn cats — generously left me diary.

News — practically none. People little communicate with each other, little discuss. Me — and outright avoid. Not just don't notice, but avoid my presence.

Some things manage to learn from servants. Judging by everything — arcadians managed to return freedom. People have another war, Masters' war, so on every little thing, like one little planet — they didn't bother to distract.

I — periodically put experiments on servants. They perceive me as goddess. Aged goddess...

So far — to kill parasite, without destroying carrier doesn't come out.

Meanwhile — I'm becoming ever weaker. Soon I simply won't be able to go out. Then me awaits death from hunger.

Actually — already even doesn't matter, will I be able to solve riddle that grandfather once solved. Anyway — no one will be to continue work.

Sometimes — I amuse myself with thoughts that maybe somewhere exists colony of earthlings, about which Masters don't know. Colony, founded by those who didn't want to carry parasite in themselves and removed it yet before launch.

Alas — I understand that these are only dreams. That technology never became universal.

My time is ending.

I write these lines... I myself don't know why. This is testimony which no one will ever read. This is tribute to memory of a man who will never be remembered.

This is monument to those who still live and, most likely, will live very, very long... But never again will become those who they were. Yes, they are immortal. They don't age. Don't fear diseases. But don't live as we lived..

Pointlessly, but...

My name is Alice. Alice Pirogova.

I — am the last mortal human on Earth.

I — am the last human on Earth.
