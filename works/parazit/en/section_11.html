Chapter 11

Today — I finally caught up with events. My granddaughter under my dictation records these lines. I — am old. I — "rejected Masters' blessing"... Hell, I was one of those who developed technology of destroying this very "blessing."

Now — I'll have to pay for it. I don't know how much longer I'll last.

At any rate, I reliably know that this morning on Earth's orbit emerged impressive fleet. All ships unknown to us. All — different.

With us made contact a certain Gaapt Sheran. Strangely — quite a humanoid himself.

State of health didn't allow me to attend negotiations. However later it was reported to me that talk went about "excessive aggressiveness of earth civilization and threat from it to whole Galaxy."

Now to meeting in Berlin — arrives their diplomatic ship. We'll be proposing military alliance against Masters. Especially as a week ago "Tereshkova" arrived with data on, presumably, mother planet of worms.

If they don't go for negotiations... Well then... Earthlings will never agree to accept foreign power or force.

Alice... Call doctors...
