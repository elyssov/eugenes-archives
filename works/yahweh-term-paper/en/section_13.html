SCENE NINE: IMAGE OR TEMPLATE

The All-Father stands by the window, staring into infinity. Silent. Then turns.

THE ALL-FATHER: Yahweh. I have a question. Basic. Fundamental.

YAHWEH: Listening!

THE ALL-FATHER: When I assigned this term paper, I said: “Create according to the TEMPLATE.” Template. Blueprint. Specification. Technical assignment. Three hundred pages of documentation you RECEIVED at the start of the semester. Remember?

YAHWEH (confidently): Of course!

THE ALL-FATHER: Then EXPLAIN to me… (points at the model) …why their NOSE looks like mine?! WHY THEIR EYES ARE SET LIKE MINE?! WHY THEY LOOK LIKE ME, BUT WORSE?!

Pause.

YAHWEH (blinking): Well… you yourself said… “in Our image”…

THE ALL-FATHER (quiet, before the explosion): What?

YAHWEH: “Let us create in Our image.” So I did! I looked at you and copied!

THE ALL-FATHER (EXPLODING): NOT IMAGE!!! TEMPLATE!!! T-E-M-P-L-A-T-E!!! ONE WORD, YAHWEH!!! I GAVE YOU BLUEPRINTS!!! DOCUMENTATION!!! INSTRUCTIONS!!! AND INSTEAD OF READING THE TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION — YOU COPIED MY FACE?!

YAHWEH (confused): But… you’re handsome, Lord Rector…

THE ALL-FATHER: I AM NOT TALKING ABOUT BEAUTY!!! I AM TALKING ABOUT COPYING APPEARANCE INSTEAD OF ARCHITECTURE!!! THAT IS WHY EVERYTHING IS BUILT THROUGH THE ASS — YOU WERE SKETCHING A PHOTO INSTEAD OF READING THE MANUAL!!!

YAHWEH (quietly): Well… during that lecture… I got a little distracted…

THE ALL-FATHER (terrible voice): You. Got. Distracted.

YAHWEH (even quieter): Baal and I… were discussing… a side project…

THE ALL-FATHER: YOU WERE WHISPERING WITH BAAL IN THE BACK ROW WHILE I WAS EXPLAINING PRINCIPLES OF BIOSPHERE DESIGN?!

YAHWEH: And with Dagon a little…

THE ALL-FATHER (freezes): And. With. Dagon.

YAHWEH: He was showing sketches of deep-sea fish. They were VERY interesting—

THE ALL-FATHER (slowly sinking into his chair, clutching his heart): Oh Go— (stops himself) …I mean, oh Me. (grabs the list) AFTER YOU, I HAVE DAGON. Yahweh. What. Did. He. Do. To the ocean.

YAHWEH (enthusiastically): Oh, you’ll LOVE it! He has an anglerfish where the male FUSES into the female and becomes a pair of testicles! And also a blobfish that looks like—

THE ALL-FATHER (shouting over him): SHUT UP!!! I DO NOT WANT TO KNOW!!! (grabs the bottle; it is empty. Turns it over. Not a drop.) MICHAEL! VODKA! ANY VODKA! BAAL’S! IMMEDIATELY!

MICHAEL (pale as a shroud): Baal only has absinthe, Lord Rector.

THE ALL-FATHER: BRING IT!!!
