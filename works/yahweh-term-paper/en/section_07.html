SCENE THREE: THE BLIND SPOT

THE ALL-FATHER (sitting, deep breath): Fine. The spleen — God… I mean — fine. Optical system. Eyes. Show me.

YAHWEH enlarges the eye model. The All-Father pushes his glasses down his nose and looks closely. First with approval. Then with a frown. Then with horror.

THE ALL-FATHER: The nerve… is routed… IN FRONT OF… the retina?

YAHWEH: There’s a small architectural nuance—

THE ALL-FATHER: A SMALL NUANCE?! YOU RAN THE CABLE OVER THE SENSOR MATRIX! THAT IS LIKE HANGING A CURTAIN IN FRONT OF A TELEVISION AND CALLING IT A “NUANCE”!

YAHWEH: But—

THE ALL-FATHER: THEY HAVE A BLIND SPOT, YAHWEH! A HOLE IN VISION! EACH OF THEM!

YAHWEH (hurrying): PATCHED IT IN SOFTWARE! The brain fills it in! They don’t even notice! Look, here’s the script: `IF blind_spot THEN move_eye AND interpolate`. Works!

THE ALL-FATHER: “WORKS”?! You know what ELSE “works”? Taping a broken chair leg with duct tape! THAT. IS. NOT. AN. ENGINEERING. SOLUTION!

YAHWEH (hurt): Well…

THE ALL-FATHER (growling): OCTOPUSES — which you submitted in FIRST YEAR — have the nerve BEHIND the retina! Correctly! No blind spot! No crutches! No “script”! AN OCTOPUS HAS A BETTER EYE THAN THE CROWN OF CREATION!

YAHWEH (even quieter): I had more time in first year…

THE ALL-FATHER: AND IN THIRD YEAR YOU WERE GETTING DRUNK AT BAAL’S!!! FOR! TWO! AEONS!!!

Michael turns the page in his notebook. His hand is shaking.
