SCENE FOURTEEN: FLORA

After the break. On the desk — three empty bottles of different origins. The All-Father sways slightly, but his eyes are clear. Michael has returned with a bruise (he collided with Dagon’s project in the hallway — something scaly and displeased).

THE ALL-FATHER: Flora. Quickly. No surprises. What. Will. They. Eat.

YAHWEH takes out a durian. Places it on the desk.

The smell instantly fills the office like nerve gas.

MICHAEL (recoiling): LORD—

THE ALL-FATHER (pinching his nose): WHAT. IS. THAT.

YAHWEH (proudly): KING OF FRUITS!

THE ALL-FATHER: IT SMELLS LIKE LUCIFER AFTER FALLING INTO A SULFUR LAKE! REMOVE IT!!!

YAHWEH: No-no-no, TRY IT! Trust me!

The All-Father, grimacing so hard his face nearly folds in half, cautiously tries the flesh. His expression changes. Slowly. Like sunrise.

THE ALL-FATHER: This is… fuck me… this is INCREDIBLY delicious. BUT WHY THE HELL DOES IT SMELL LIKE THAT?!

YAHWEH: QUEST, LORD RECTOR! TRIAL! OVERCOMING FEAR! TRUST IN THE UNKNOWN!

THE ALL-FATHER: YOU COULD HAVE JUST MADE IT DELICIOUS! WITHOUT THE SMELL OF A DEAD DEMON!

YAHWEH: I COULD! BUT THEN IT WOULDN’T BE INTERESTING!

YAHWEH takes out an eggplant. Places it on the desk. Silently.

THE ALL-FATHER (looking at its shape): No.

YAHWEH (innocently): It’s a vegetable!

THE ALL-FATHER: Yahweh. I SEE its shape. I see its SIZE. I see its COLOR. IT LOOKS LIKE—

YAHWEH (with absolutely pure eyes): Like WHAT, Lord Rector?

THE ALL-FATHER: …

YAHWEH: It is just a VEGETABLE! Healthy eating! Vitamins! Fiber!

THE ALL-FATHER: THEY WILL USE IT AS AN EMOJI FOR DICK, YAHWEH!

YAHWEH: What is an “emoji”?

THE ALL-FATHER: YOU’LL FIND OUT IN A FEW THOUSAND YEARS! WHEN YOU SEE WHAT THEY DO WITH YOUR “VEGETABLE”!

MICHAEL (writing): “Eggplant. Shape unambiguously phallic. Student categorically denies intent. Rector categorically does not believe him. Refer matter to the Semiotics Committee.”
