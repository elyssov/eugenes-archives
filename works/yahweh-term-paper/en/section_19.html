SCENE FIFTEEN: THE LAMB

Yahweh reaches into the box for the next exhibit. The All-Father raises a hand. Silent.

Long silence.

He sits. Removes his glasses. Rubs his eyes. When he speaks, his voice is different. Quiet. No shouting. No sarcasm. Tired — but not from the defense. From something larger.

THE ALL-FATHER: Yahweh. Sit.

Yahweh sits. For the first time in the whole defense, he feels the jokes are over.

THE ALL-FATHER: I have reviewed the whole project. All the fauna. All the flora. Anatomy. Blind spot, sewer, belly bomb, hippo, Sacculina. Everything.

Pause.

THE ALL-FATHER: I have one question. One.

Pause.

THE ALL-FATHER: Why so much pain?

Yahweh opens his mouth. Closes it.

THE ALL-FATHER: A lamb. A little lamb. A lion runs after it. The lamb SCREAMS. It feels terror. Pure, absolute, paralyzing terror. Then teeth enter the body. And it feels PAIN. Every second. Every nerve sends the signal. Until it dies. And it does not die immediately.

Silence. Michael does not write.

THE ALL-FATHER: It did not eat from the tree. It did not break commandments. It has no free will. It did not CHOOSE. Did not sin. Could not sin. It is just a lamb. (pause; his voice cracks) Why does it hurt, Yahweh?

Yahweh is silent.

THE ALL-FATHER: Why did you BUILD a nervous system that transmits suffering into a creature that can DO NOTHING WITH IT? Why does a doe WEEP over a dead fawn? (quietly, almost a whisper) Why does a crab CARE for a parasite’s eggs and not know its life is over? You were PROUD of that. You SHONE. You found it BEAUTIFUL.

Pause. The longest pause in the history of the Academy.

YAHWEH (voice small and quiet, nothing like the one that was just shouting about Sacculina): So that… it suffers more sufferingly.

Silence.

THE ALL-FATHER: What?

YAHWEH (even quieter, looking at the floor): I’m sorry. I don’t know. It just… (pause) …came out more interesting. I wasn’t thinking about the lamb. I was thinking about… (cannot find the word) …the system. Balance. Food chains. Predator — prey. Energy — biomass… I didn’t… (trails off)

Very long silence.

THE ALL-FATHER (standing): There. (walks to the window) That is the problem, Yahweh. Not the blind spot. Not the sewer. Not the baculum. Those are minor things. Curiosities. Funny, offensive, ridiculous. But MINOR.

(Turns.)

You created a world where suffering is a FUNCTION. Not a side effect. Not a bug. A FUNCTION. The lamb screams because you PROGRAMMED it to scream. The crab loves someone else’s children because you PROGRAMMED it to love. Innocent pain is not a design error. It is DESIGN.

YAHWEH (not raising his eyes): I didn’t want—

THE ALL-FATHER: You did. Sacculina was not an accident. You took it from a HIDDEN POCKET. You saved it for last. You are PROUD of it.

Pause.

THE ALL-FATHER: And do you know the most terrifying part?

Yahweh slowly lifts his eyes.

THE ALL-FATHER: The project is ACCEPTED. Because it is ALIVE. Because it contains what sterile, painless, dead worlds from the Department of Determinism do not have. Chaos. Freedom. Unpredictability. Laughter. Love. And — yes — pain.

Pause.

THE ALL-FATHER (quietly, looking Yahweh in the eye): But I will remember the lamb. And so will you. Every time you look at your world — remember the little lamb that hurts. For no reason. Simply because you gave it a nervous system and forgot to ask whether it WANTED to feel.
