SCENE SEVEN: THE BOMB IN THE BELLY

The All-Father, creaking like an old oak in a storm, leans toward the intestinal model. Snaps his fingers — the intestines unfold like an endless garland.

THE ALL-FATHER: Why so long?

YAHWEH: Better absorption!

THE ALL-FATHER: Eight meters, Yahweh. EIGHT METERS of guts in one human. (points) And what is this little—

His finger lands on a small blind pouch. Unremarkable. Dead-ended.

YAHWEH (straightening like a man receiving a Nobel Prize): APPENDIX!

THE ALL-FATHER: Function?

YAHWEH: BUNKER! Listen: one day they’ll invent antibiotics. Antibiotics will SCORCH the entire gut microbiome. Total purge. But! Inside the appendix — BACKUP COPY! Reserve garrison of beneficial bacteria! They ride out the chemical attack and restore the population! Like a bomb shelter, but for microbes!

Pause. The All-Father removes his glasses. Cleans them. Puts them back on. Looks at the appendix.

THE ALL-FATHER (reluctantly): That… is actually… reasonable.

Yahweh BLOOMS. Literally — microscopic flowers start blooming around him, a side effect of divine joy. Michael sneezes from the pollen.

THE ALL-FATHER: Did you include face control at the bunker entrance?

The flowers instantly wilt.

YAHWEH: …Meaning?

THE ALL-FATHER: HOW DOES THE BUNKER DECIDE WHO TO LET IN? The locals — useful lactobacilli, bifidobacteria? Or saboteurs — pathogenic scum that will stage a MUTINY?

YAHWEH (barely audible): On… their honor?

Silence. Michael stops writing. Even the frog, which has been hiding under the desk this whole time, freezes.

THE ALL-FATHER: ON. THEIR. FUCKING. HONOR.

YAHWEH (quickly): Well, not JUST any microbe can get in! There’s a… well… mucous membrane at the entrance… it sort of—

THE ALL-FATHER: SORT OF WHAT? SORT OF FILTERS? SORT OF ADMITS? SORT OF “GOOD ENOUGH”?

YAHWEH: Most normal bacteria will figure it out themselves!

THE ALL-FATHER: AND THE ABNORMAL ONES?!

YAHWEH (instantly perking up): Oh! THEN AN INFLAMMATORY STORM BEGINS! Classic alarm signal! Fever, pain, leukocytes storming the bunker—

THE ALL-FATHER: AND HOW DOES THIS STORM END?!

YAHWEH (a little quieter): …Sometimes surgery.

THE ALL-FATHER (half-standing, hands pressed to the desk so hard fossilized time CREAKS): YOU! INSTALLED! A TIME! BOMB! IN! THE! ABDOMINAL CAVITY! OF! EVERY HUMAN! WITH FACE CONTROL BASED ON “TRUST ME BRO”! AND WHEN THE BOMB GOES OFF — YOU NEED A SURGEON WITH A KNIFE!

YAHWEH (with a martyr’s smile): But what DRAMA, Lord Rector!

THE ALL-FATHER (to Michael): VALERIAN! TRIPLE!

MICHAEL (pale): There’s only a little left at the bottom, Lord Rector.

THE ALL-FATHER: WE ARE ONLY HALFWAY THROUGH THE DEFENSE!!!

MICHAEL (even paler): I know.
