SCENE ONE: THE ENTRANCE

14:47 absolute divine time. Yahweh is late. The All-Father sits with his eyes closed. Michael sharpens a pencil. Silence.

The door is KICKED open. YAHWEH bursts in. His box slams into the doorframe. A frog jumps out, runs across the All-Father’s desk between the bottle and the stack of term papers, knocks over a cup of pencils, and disappears under the Rector’s robe.

YAHWEH (not noticing the frog, not noticing the pencils, not noticing the All-Father’s face): LORD RECTOR! I! AM! READY! FOR! DEFENSE!

Pause. The frog crawls up the All-Father’s leg.

THE ALL-FATHER (without moving): You are two aeons late.

YAHWEH: Baal. Party. You know Baal — “I’ll just drop by for five minutes” turns into…

THE ALL-FATHER: Two aeons. Yes. I know Baal.

YAHWEH: But I FINISHED EVERYTHING! I was literally putting in the final touches on the way here!

THE ALL-FATHER (sniffing): You smell of sulfur, Red Bull, and… burning?

YAHWEH: That was an experiment! Don’t worry! We put it out!

THE ALL-FATHER (slowly): What. Exactly. Did you put out.

YAHWEH: …Not important. Minor thing. Creative process overhead.

MICHAEL (writing): “14:47. Student Yahweh. Late by two aeons. Smell: sulfur, energy drinks, burning. Something burned. Student claims it was extinguished. Do not ask.”

THE ALL-FATHER: Term paper topic: “Closed-Type Biosphere on a Rocky Planet, Third from a G2V-Class Star.” Deadline: two aeons ago. Show me. (pause) And remove the frog from under my robe.

YAHWEH: Oh! It got out by itself? Prototype amphibian! See how lively? Breathes with lungs and skin, reproduces in water but lives on land—

THE ALL-FATHER: Yahweh. Frog. From under my robe. Now.

YAHWEH (catching the frog): By the way, it has a unique visual system — it sees movement but not static objects! If a fly freezes, the frog loses it!

THE ALL-FATHER: You made a predator that LOSES its prey if the prey stops moving?

YAHWEH: …It’s a feature.

THE ALL-FATHER (to Michael): Write that down. First minute of the defense. We already have “feature.”
