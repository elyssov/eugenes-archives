SETTING

The Rector’s office at the Higher Divine Academy. The ceilings disappear into infinity — literally, not metaphorically. The desk is made of fossilized time, a gift from the Department of Chronodynamics, heavy as fate. On the wall: diplomas, certificates, and one photograph — the young All-Father shaking hands with Chaos at graduation. Both are smiling. Chaos does not have all his hands. The All-Father does not have all his illusions.

A nameplate on the desk: “UNTIL RETIREMENT: ∞.” A second nameplate, handwritten: “AND YOU CAN FEEL IT.” A third, smallest one, taped on by Michael: “YAHWEH — 14:00.”
