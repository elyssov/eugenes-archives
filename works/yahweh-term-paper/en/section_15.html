SCENE ELEVEN: THE ZOO

The All-Father has drunk the absinthe. His eyes are slightly glassy, but his capacity for horror remains intact.

THE ALL-FATHER: Fauna. Quickly. What is in the box.

YAHWEH opens the box. A many-voiced roar, hiss, and something like croaking — but EVIL — comes from inside.

YAHWEH (pulling something out): HIPPOPOTAMUS!

THE ALL-FATHER: Herbivore?

YAHWEH: YES!

THE ALL-FATHER (reading): Two tons. Runs fifty kilometers per hour. Kills more people than crocodiles, lions, and sharks COMBINED. Territorial aggression. Can bite a crocodile IN HALF.

YAHWEH: Well, slightly temperamental…

THE ALL-FATHER: “SLIGHTLY TEMPERAMENTAL”?! YOU PUT AN ARMORED TRAIN IN THE COW LINEUP AND CALLED IT A “HERBIVORE”! IT EATS GRASS AND KILLS EVERYTHING THAT MOVES! WHY?!

YAHWEH: For balance!

THE ALL-FATHER: WHAT FUCKING BALANCE?! WHO BALANCES A TWO-TON PSYCHOPATH WITH A MOUTH BIG ENOUGH TO HOLD A WATERMELON?!

YAHWEH (thoughtfully): …Another hippo?

The All-Father grabs his head.

YAHWEH (quickly pulling out the next thing): AND HERE — PLATYPUS!

The platypus blinks. It has a bill. And spurs on its hind legs.

THE ALL-FATHER: Class?

YAHWEH: Mammal!

THE ALL-FATHER: With a bill.

YAHWEH: Yes!

THE ALL-FATHER: Lays eggs.

YAHWEH: Yes!

THE ALL-FATHER: Venomous.

YAHWEH: Yes!

THE ALL-FATHER: Electroreception.

YAHWEH: …How do you know?!

THE ALL-FATHER: LOKI HELPED, DIDN’T HE?!

YAHWEH (turning pale): How—

THE ALL-FATHER (growling): BECAUSE ONLY LOKI COULD LOOK AT A DUCK, A BEAVER, A SNAKE, AND AN ELECTRIC EEL AND SAY: “LET’S GLUE THEM TOGETHER.” Did you SHOW HIM THE PROJECT?!

YAHWEH (very quietly): He… gave it a B.

THE ALL-FATHER: A B?! LOKI?! FOR THIS?! (to Michael) WRITE IT DOWN!!! STUDENT CONSULTED LOKI!!! THAT! EXPLAINS! A LOT!!!
