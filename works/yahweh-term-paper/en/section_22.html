P.S. — Somewhere in the hallway of the Higher Divine Academy, student Yahweh, smeared with coffee and delight, sits on a windowsill sketching the outline of his thesis. Working title: “Black Holes: Feature or Bug?” Beside him — Loki, offering suggestions. Beside Loki — Dagon, showing sketches.

Michael walks past. Glances in. Sees the drawing. Turns pale.

Yahweh, without turning, grinning ear to ear:

“For.”
