SCENE THIRTEEN: SELF-PROPELLED

YAHWEH (now utterly UNHINGED with inspiration, winding himself up like a hurricane): AND HERE’S ANOTHER ONE!!!

THE ALL-FATHER (doomed): What.

YAHWEH: OCTOPUS! Remember first year?

THE ALL-FATHER: Nine brains. I remember.

YAHWEH: That was BETA! THIS IS RELEASE! Each tentacle is AUTONOMOUS! Its own nervous system! Thinks by itself, decides by itself, acts by itself! The central brain only COORDINATES! Distributed intelligence!

THE ALL-FATHER (despite everything): That… is a good idea. Seriously.

YAHWEH (freezing like a puppy being petted): …Really?

THE ALL-FATHER: Really. Distributed processing. Fault tolerance. Parallel computation. Well done.

Yahweh stands stunned by praise. His eyes are wet. His lower lip trembles. He looks ready to cry from happiness.

And exactly at THIS moment — the one moment of praise in the entire defense — Yahweh leans toward the All-Father with the expression Michael has learned to recognize and FEAR:

YAHWEH (whispering like a conspirator): And also. In some species. The penis comes off.

Silence.

Cosmic.

Absolute.

The kind of silence in which you can hear hope dying.

THE ALL-FATHER (broken voice): …What. Did. You. Say.

YAHWEH (getting louder): COMES OFF! BY ITSELF! Separates from the body! SWIMS! Independently! SEEKS A FEMALE! Without navigation! FINDS HER! AND MATES! WITHOUT THE OWNER PRESENT!

The All-Father stares at one point. Glassy eyes. Mouth slightly open. Hands lying lifeless on the desk.

THE ALL-FATHER (slowly, syllable by syllable, like a man realizing the bottom has been broken and below it there is another bottom, and another): Self-propelled… dick…

YAHWEH (scientifically enthusiastic): Hectocotylus! I gave it a beautiful scientific name!

THE ALL-FATHER (not hearing him): Self-propelled. Dick. Autonomous. With a guidance system. (pause) Now. I have seen. Everything.

YAHWEH (nudging the box with his foot; suspicious hissing comes from inside): Actually, I’ve only just started—

THE ALL-FATHER (LEAPING UP, SLAMMING BOTH FISTS ON THE DESK SO HARD THE PLATYPUS JUMPS, AN EMPTY BOTTLE SHATTERS, AND THE FROG FLIES SCREAMING INTO THE HALLWAY): BREAK!!! TEN MINUTES!!! MICHAEL!!! VODKA!!! ABSINTHE!!! VALERIAN!!! EVERYTHING WE HAVE!!! FROM BAAL!!! FROM DAGON!!! FROM LUCIFER HIMSELF IF NECESSARY!!!

MICHAEL (grabbing his notebook and backing toward the door): At once, Lord Rector!

THE ALL-FATHER (after him): AND TELL DAGON TO WAIT! I. AM. NOT. READY.

From the hallway — Michael’s retreating footsteps and Dagon’s approaching shout: “I’ve got a fish that walks on land!”

The All-Father slowly sits. Closes his eyes. Whispers:

THE ALL-FATHER: Self-propelled dick… Why did I become a rector…
