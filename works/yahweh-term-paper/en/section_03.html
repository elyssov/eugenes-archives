DRAMATIS PERSONAE

THE ALL-FATHER — Rector of the Higher Divine Academy. Created universes, survived Lucifer, survived Azazel’s term paper on nuclear physics (it got hot), but was not prepared for THIS. Robe the color of dark matter. Bags under his eyes the size of the Andromeda Nebula. On his desk: a bottle of Celestial Valerian (opened before the defense even began), today’s student list (Yahweh, Dagon, Moloch — in ascending order of horror), and a nameplate reading: “UNTIL RETIREMENT: ∞.”

YAHWEH — Third-year student at the Faculty of Applied Worldbuilding. Specialization: biospheres. Has not slept for six days. His eyes burn bright enough to ignite stars. His lab coat is stained with coffee, ketchup, and something better left unidentified (he swears it is tomato juice; Michael does not believe him). Drags around a cardboard box from which noises periodically emerge that make Michael cross himself involuntarily — and he is an ARCHANGEL, so officially he is not supposed to.

MICHAEL — Archangel, personal secretary to the All-Father. Takes minutes. The only being in the Universe who is DOCUMENTING this shitshow, and therefore the only one who will later have to explain to the Ethics Committee how this was allowed to happen. His wings are trembling. His supply of notebooks is almost gone. His supply of nerves has been gone for ages.
