SCENE TWO: THE CROWN OF CREATION

YAHWEH snaps his fingers with the look of a magician about to pull out a rabbit. A holographic human model materializes in the air. To be fair — it is beautiful. Anatomically detailed. Glowing with soft golden light. The All-Father puts on his glasses. Michael leans closer.

THE ALL-FATHER: Central model. “Crown of creation,” as you wrote in the abstract.

YAHWEH: Twenty-one times!

THE ALL-FATHER: What?

YAHWEH: I wrote “crown of creation” twenty-one times. With an exclamation mark. Every time. Plus once on the title page, separate font, gold letters.

THE ALL-FATHER: Yahweh, modesty—

YAHWEH: —is the virtue of the weak, Lord Rector! And I am a CREATOR!

MICHAEL (writing): “Student calls himself creator. Remind him about hierarchy. After the defense. If we survive.”

THE ALL-FATHER: Open the anatomy. Let us see what your “crown” is made of.

Yahweh waves a hand. The model unfolds like an anatomical construction kit: organs suspended in the air, each in its place, illuminated and labeled. It looks impressive. For five seconds.

THE ALL-FATHER (leaning in): Stop. (points at a dark-red thing) What the hell is this?

YAHWEH (instantly excited): Spleen!

THE ALL-FATHER: Function?

YAHWEH: Filters blood, destroys old red blood cells, stores platelets, helps fight infections—

THE ALL-FATHER: And if you remove it?

Pause. Yahweh blinks.

YAHWEH: …Well…

THE ALL-FATHER: Yahweh. Simple question. If this organ is REMOVED — does the organism die?

YAHWEH (very quietly): …They can live without it.

THE ALL-FATHER (rising): YOU PUT A SPARE ORGAN INTO THE CROWN OF CREATION!

YAHWEH: There were parts left in the box! I thought — what if it comes in handy! Backup option!

THE ALL-FATHER: “WHAT IF IT COMES IN HANDY”?! Yahweh, this is a HUMAN BEING, not a SHED where you dump everything you felt bad throwing away!

YAHWEH (muttering): Better to have it and not need it than need it and not have it…

THE ALL-FATHER: THAT IS NOT AN ARGUMENT FOR ANATOMY!

MICHAEL (writing): “Spare organ. Motivation: ‘parts left in the box.’ Rector stood up. Voice elevated.”
