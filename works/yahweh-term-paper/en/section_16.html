SCENE TWELVE: SACCULINA

Silence. Yahweh slowly, carefully, TENDERLY takes a small container from the very bottom of the box. Not the main compartment. A hidden pocket. Like the greatest treasure.

His hands are not shaking. His hands are GLOWING.

YAHWEH (and his voice is different — quiet, almost intimate): Lord Rector. Everything you have seen so far… (pause) …was a warm-up.

The All-Father feels the change. Straightens. Michael stops writing. The room becomes COLD.

YAHWEH: This. Is. My. True. Pride.

He opens the container. Inside is a tiny, almost invisible creature. It looks like a disposable biological syringe. Nothing special. If you do not know.

THE ALL-FATHER: A little crustacean?

YAHWEH (shakes his head): No. A SYSTEM.

THE ALL-FATHER: Explain.

YAHWEH (starts quietly, then louder and faster with each word, unstoppable): From the egg hatches THIS. Self-propelled. Biological. Syringe. Not metaphorically — LITERALLY a syringe. It hatches — and IMMEDIATELY knows what to look for. ONLY crabs. Not fish. Not jellyfish. Not stars. It has a guidance system that WORKS OUT OF THE BOX. No training. No experience. NEWBORN — and already a hunter.

THE ALL-FATHER: Continue.

YAHWEH: It finds the crab. Finds a VULNERABLE POINT — leg joint, scar in the shell. It doesn’t poke randomly! IT KNOWS THE VICTIM’S ANATOMY! Then — it inserts itself…

Yahweh pauses. Not for effect. No. He is ENJOYING the moment.

YAHWEH: …and INJECTS. ITSELF. WHOLE.

Silence like ringing glass.

THE ALL-FATHER (slowly): Repeat that.

YAHWEH (eyes burning): The ENTIRE organism. Inside the crab. Outside — empty shell. Used syringe. TRASH. Inside — undifferentiated cells, CLOSELY RELATED to host cells. Immune system DOES NOT SEE. No rejection. No inflammation. No alarm signal. NOTHING. Invisible.

THE ALL-FATHER (pushing his chair back): Continue.

YAHWEH (he cannot stop now): The cells grow through EVERY organ. EVERY tissue. Every joint. The parasite becomes PART OF THE HOST BODY. Then — CASTRATES. Chemically. Shuts down the crab’s reproductive system COMPLETELY.

THE ALL-FATHER: Enough.

YAHWEH: THEN! IT CHANGES! THE HORMONAL! PROFILE!

THE ALL-FATHER: I SAID ENOUGH!

YAHWEH (shouting): IF THE HOST IS MALE — SHE MAKES HIM FEMALE!!! HE WIDENS HIS ABDOMEN!!! PREPARES TO BROOD!!! CARES FOR HER EGGS!!! VENTILATES THEM!!! PROTECTS THEM!!! HE THINKS THEY ARE HIS CHILDREN!!! HE LOVES THEM!!!

THE ALL-FATHER slowly stands. Slowly raises his hands. Slowly ANNIHILATES them. Slowly recreates them. Sits down.

THE ALL-FATHER (voice from the grave): Yahweh. How. Did. You. Think of this.

YAHWEH (SHINING LIKE A FUCKING SUPERNOVA, LIKE A CHRISTMAS TREE, LIKE A CHILD WHO HAS RECEIVED EVERY PRESENT IN THE WORLD): ARE YOU PROUD OF ME, LORD RECTOR?!?!

The All-Father performs a double facepalm so powerful the lights in the office go out. Then they return. From behind his hands comes a long, endless groan like the hum of a dying universe.

THE ALL-FATHER: Yaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaahweeeeeeeeeeeeeh…

YAHWEH (ABSOLUTELY not noticing the Rector’s agony): AND DO YOU KNOW THE KILLER FEATURE?!?!

THE ALL-FATHER (behind his hands): No. I do not. I do not want to. I beg you. No.

YAHWEH: IT. DOES. NOT. CARE. WHAT. SEX. THE HOST. IS!!! MALE, FEMALE — NO DIFFERENCE!!! BECAUSE IT CAN!!!

Long, infinite silence. Michael sits with pen raised. On the notebook page is one word, large, crooked, written by a trembling hand: “WHY.”

THE ALL-FATHER (lowering his hands, looking at Yahweh): You have just described the perfect mechanism of enslavement. The victim is alive. The victim functions. The victim THINKS everything is normal. The victim is HAPPY. It cares for the offspring of its murderer. It LOVES its murderer. (pause) This is the most terrifying thing I have seen in the entire history of the Academy. And I have seen Lucifer’s thesis.

YAHWEH (waving it off): Pff! Lucifer! Fire from the ground! Banal! Stupid! No elegance, no subtext! But this—

THE ALL-FATHER (cutting him off): ELEGANCE?! YOU CALL THIS ELEGANCE?!

YAHWEH (with absolute, unbreakable, GRANITE certainty): Yes. It is elegant. It is beautiful. It is PERFECT.

Pause. The All-Father looks at Yahweh. And for the first time in the whole defense, he sees something in his eyes he cannot classify: genius, madness, or some third thing for which no name yet exists.

THE ALL-FATHER (to Michael, whispering): Double valerian. And call the Ethics Committee. And the Dean’s Office. (pause) And ask Baal whether he has anything stronger than absinthe.
