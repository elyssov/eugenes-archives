SCENE FOUR: UPRIGHT WALKING

THE ALL-FATHER (massaging his temples): Fine. Eyes — fuck it. Musculoskeletal system. I see you put them on two legs?

YAHWEH (proudly): Upright walking! Frees the hands! They can use tools!

THE ALL-FATHER: Beautiful idea. Let’s see the implementation. (flips pages) So… spine. (pause) Yahweh. This is a QUADRUPED spine.

YAHWEH: Well—

THE ALL-FATHER: You took a horizontal bridge structure designed for distributed load and STOOD IT UPRIGHT. That is like taking a bridge over a river and sticking it into the ground as a pole.

YAHWEH: I added curves! Lordosis, kyphosis — shock absorption!

THE ALL-FATHER: SHOCK ABSORPTION?! THEIR INTERVERTEBRAL DISCS WILL START POPPING BY FORTY! Hernias, protrusions, osteochondrosis… Did you even CALCULATE the load?!

YAHWEH (pause): …I did! In general.

THE ALL-FATHER: “In general.” (reads further) Knee joint. Yahweh. This is a joint FOR A QUADRUPED. The meniscus is designed for HORIZONTAL loading. And you loaded it VERTICALLY. With the whole body weight. On TWO SUPPORT POINTS INSTEAD OF FOUR.

YAHWEH: Menisci are strong! Cartilage!

THE ALL-FATHER: CARTILAGE THAT DOES NOT REGENERATE! Tear it — done! You made a load-bearing component out of material THAT CANNOT BE REPAIRED!

YAHWEH: Well… they’ll be careful?

THE ALL-FATHER (standing, pacing): THEY’LL BE CAREFUL?! You made them run, jump, climb — and told their knees “be careful”?! That is like giving matches to a child and saying “don’t set anything on fire”!

YAHWEH: Will children have matches too?

THE ALL-FATHER: DON’T CHANGE THE SUBJECT! (points at the model) Varicose veins! Do you know what that is? Blood has to climb from the feet to the heart STRAIGHT UP. Against gravity! Did you at least install proper VALVES?

YAHWEH: …Valves? (rummages through the spec) Ah. Yes. Valves. Somewhere…

THE ALL-FATHER: “SOMEWHERE”! HALF OF THEM WILL HAVE BLUE LUMPY LEGS BY FIFTY BECAUSE YOU DID NOT THINK THROUGH VENOUS RETURN!

YAHWEH (quietly, with dignity): But they will be able to LOOK AT THE STARS. Standing. Head tilted back. No quadruped can do that.

Pause.

THE ALL-FATHER (stopping short): …That… (sighs) …fine. Fine. Beautifully said. But redo the spine anyway.

YAHWEH: After the defense!

THE ALL-FATHER: NO, NOT AFTER— (waves him off) Continue.
