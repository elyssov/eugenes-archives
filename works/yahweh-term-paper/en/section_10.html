SCENE SIX: BACULUM, OR THE GREAT DRAMA

THE ALL-FATHER (pouring valerian down his throat, wiping his mouth with his robe): Right. Walrus — baculum. Bone in the sexual organ. Dog — has it. Bear — has it. Most large mammals — have it. Stability. Reliability. Confidence. (raises his eyes to Yahweh) And the CROWN. OF CREATION. Does not. Why.

Yahweh is silent for exactly one second. Then his face lights up. THAT smile. Michael sees it and begins writing PREEMPTIVELY: “Major problem. Details below.”

YAHWEH (solemnly, like a prophet revealing truth): Imagine. The level. Of DRAMA. From impotence.

Silence.

Absolute, primordial, pre-biblical silence.

Somewhere in the hallway of the Academy, someone drops a planet. No one notices.

THE ALL-FATHER (whispering): What. Did. You. Say.

YAHWEH (gaining momentum like a locomotive whose brakes have failed): They will SUFFER! WORRY! Lie awake at night and THINK! Write SONNETS! POEMS! Entire PHILOSOPHICAL SCHOOLS will grow around the limp dick! Men will buy fast chariots to COMPENSATE! It’s the ENGINE OF PROGRESS, Lord Rector! CIVILIZATIONS WILL GROW out of this insecurity!

THE ALL-FATHER (slowly rising): You. Removed. The bone. From. Your. Main. Species’. DICK. For. Fucking. SONNETS.

YAHWEH: AND ECONOMICS! In a few thousand years they’ll invent Viagra — a BILLION-DOLLAR business! Perfect arc: from suffering to pharmaceutical breakthrough!

THE ALL-FATHER: YOU BUILT IMPOTENCE IN AS A BUSINESS MODEL?!

YAHWEH (dead serious): Venture-capital anatomy, Lord Rector. Create the problem — sell the solution.

The All-Father opens his mouth. Closes it. Opens it again. Nothing comes out.

THE ALL-FATHER (to Michael, strangled): Write this down. Baculum. For everyone. No exceptions. NEXT semester. UNDERLINE it.

MICHAEL (writing): “Baculum. Underlined. Circled. Three exclamation marks. Rector turning crimson.”
