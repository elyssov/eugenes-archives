SCENE TEN: HERMAPHRODITE

While Michael runs for absinthe, Yahweh tries to HIDE something behind his back. A slippery, elegant, incomprehensible thing slips from his fingers, rolls across the desk, and FLIES into the unfolded holographic model of the planet, landing somewhere in Southeast Asia.

The All-Father follows its trajectory with the expression of a man watching a meteorite fall into his own house. Slowly turns to Yahweh.

Silence. Long silence.

YAHWEH (hands raised): Experimental model! For balancing genetic diversity in certain regions!

THE ALL-FATHER: It. Landed. In Thailand.

YAHWEH: …For tourism?

MICHAEL (returning with absinthe, writing): “Hermaphrodite. Thailand. Tourism. Never show this line to the Ethics Committee.”
