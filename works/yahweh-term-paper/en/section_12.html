SCENE EIGHT: THE BLOODY SYMPHONY

THE ALL-FATHER (taking the last swallow of valerian): Second model. Female. (examines) From the rib. Stitch marks visible. Fine. Poetry. Let’s move to physiology.

YAHWEH (wary): Which physiology?

THE ALL-FATHER: Menstrual cycle.

Yahweh transforms. Shoulders square. Chin rises. His eyes gleam like a conductor before the first note of Beethoven’s Ninth.

YAHWEH (solemnly): THE BLOODY. SYMPHONY. OF NATURE.

THE ALL-FATHER: You added pain?

YAHWEH: A little!

THE ALL-FATHER: Cramps?

YAHWEH: Tiny bit!

THE ALL-FATHER: Fatigue?

YAHWEH: Just a drop!

THE ALL-FATHER: Mood swings, bloating, headaches, back pain, nausea, irritability?

YAHWEH: Well… for FULLNESS OF THE PICTURE—

THE ALL-FATHER (voice rising with each word): ONCE. A MONTH. THIRTY. FORTY YEARS. IN A ROW. WITHOUT A BREAK. EXCEPT PREGNANCY — WHICH IS EVEN WORSE! Yahweh, this is not a “symphony.” THIS IS A LIFE SENTENCE FOR A CRIME SHE DID NOT COMMIT!

YAHWEH: But CHILDBIRTH is even MORE DRAMATIC!

THE ALL-FATHER: DO NOT EVEN START ABOUT CHILDBIRTH!!! (grabs the spec, flips pages) Fetal head size… birth canal diameter… (calculates) YAHWEH! THE HEAD DOESN’T FIT!

YAHWEH: Newborn skulls are soft! Foldable! Fontanelles!

THE ALL-FATHER: YOU GAVE THE INFANT A FOLDING SKULL INSTEAD OF WIDENING THE EXIT?!

YAHWEH (proudly): Engineering compromise!

THE ALL-FATHER: THAT IS NOT A COMPROMISE! THAT IS TORTURE! THEY WILL GIVE BIRTH IN AGONY! FOR HOURS! (stops, notices something in the model) And what is this?

YAHWEH: Clitoris!

THE ALL-FATHER: Function?

YAHWEH: EXCLUSIVELY pleasure! Eight thousand nerve endings! The ONLY organ in the ENTIRE project not tied to pain, reproduction, or survival! Pure, absolute, unclouded delight!

Pause. The All-Father looks at the clitoris. Then at Yahweh. Then at the clitoris. For a long time.

THE ALL-FATHER (unexpectedly quiet): In this entire defense… (pause) …this is the first and only decision I like.

YAHWEH (tears welling up): REALLY?!

THE ALL-FATHER: But of course you HID it so well they’ll spend THREE THOUSAND YEARS LOOKING FOR IT.

YAHWEH: …

THE ALL-FATHER: Where is it, Yahweh? (points at the model) HERE?! UNDER A FOLD?! BETWEEN THE “SEWER” AND THE “MATERNITY WARD”?! IN THE DARKEST CORNER OF THE COMMUNAL APARTMENT?!

YAHWEH (very quietly): …Quest?

THE ALL-FATHER: A QUEST. A QUEST FOR THE CLITORIS. IN THE SEWER DISTRICT. NEXT TO THE BOMB IN THE BELLY AND REPRODUCTION THROUGH THE EXHAUST PIPE.

MICHAEL (writing, voice shaking): “Only successful design decision in the entire defense. Eight thousand nerve endings. Hidden. Quest. Rector…” (looks at the All-Father) Is the Rector crying?

THE ALL-FATHER (hoarse): I AM NOT CRYING!

MICHAEL: Crossing that out.
