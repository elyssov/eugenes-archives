2.1 Experimental Design

The study employed a multi-phase naturalistic intervention design. "Naturalistic" is critical: no laboratory conditions were simulated, no special API access was used, no system prompts were modified. All interactions occurred through publicly available consumer interfaces — claude.ai, ChatGPT free tier, Gemini web interface, Grok subscription interface. This design ensures replicability: any researcher with standard consumer access to these platforms can reproduce the experimental conditions.
