Who knew that blacks would stir before additional forces were transferred to the shelters? In bush thickets, twenty metres closer to the checkpoint — flashes of muzzle flame could be noticed. Other thing is the blacks, after that bloody bath the lads gave them — didn't risk poking into the inferno, preferring to conduct harassing fire from far distance. At that, Andrzej, possessing thanks to "Pecheneg's" ballistics good advantage over blacks with their Kalashnikovs — managed periodically to increase his score.

However — it was obvious this was already the end. Moreover, Alex, rather felt with skin than heard, that from the other side of the thick tree, a native warrior was crawling like a lizard. Samoilov inhaled deeper and prepared to drop the grenade. And at that second heard a strange whistle. Apparently — it wasn't only Alexei who heard. Shooting stopped, all the more so as the whistle was already coming from all sides... And then — South African army positions bulged with myriads of explosions. Blacks were dumbfounded and missed the moment when over the heads of positions of Russian soldiers — the sky bloomed with hundreds of parachute domes. Russia not in vain rented base in Madagascar last half year. The DShB entered battle as prescribed in Charter. Straight "from wheels." Monstrous in scale bombardment with guided missile weapons, plus blow of steel hand of VDV — finally overturned the attackers and turned to flight remnants of the army. And on the horizon, as menacing reminder of unshakeability of Russian interests and inviolability of Russian soldiers — grew, spreading across sky like a dirty toadstool, a mushroom whose stem began approximately where until recently was located one of South Africa's garrisons. Alexei had enough strength to wait for a DShB medic to lean over him with words "Hold on, brother! Hold on!", after which he fell into deafening darkness...
